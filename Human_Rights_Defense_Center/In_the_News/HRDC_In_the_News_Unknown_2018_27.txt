HRDC files censorship suit against Virginia jail
2018
by The Associated Press Friday, April 6th 2018  ABINGDON, Va. — The publisher of a magazine geared toward prisoners is suing a southwest Virginia jail for blocking inmates from receiving its publications. The center publishes Prison Legal News, a monthly magazine that covers prisoners' rights issues and other legal material. Its editor is a former prisoner. The suit says the jail's policies regarding outside reading materials are arbitrary. Jail officials did not return a call seeking comment.
