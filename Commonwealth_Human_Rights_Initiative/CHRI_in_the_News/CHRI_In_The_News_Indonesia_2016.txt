
Latest Round of Executions in Indonesia for Drug-related OffensesCH
2016
Letter to the President of Indonesia by Global South-based human rights NGOs– Asian Forum for Human Rights and Development (FORUM-ASIA), Center for Human Rights of the University of Pretoria, Comisión Mexicana de Defensa y Promoción de los Derechos Humanos, Commonwealth Human Rights Initiative, Conectas Human Rights and Partnership for Justice– to express grave concerns for the impending round of executions of Indonesian and foreign nationals, convicted in Indonesia for drug-related offenses.
