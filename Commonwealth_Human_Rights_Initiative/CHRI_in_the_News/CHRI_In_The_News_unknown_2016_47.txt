
Fate Of Rohingyas In Bengal Prisons Hangs In BalanceCH
2016
KOLKATA -- Over 80 Rohingya Muslims lodged in various prisons across Bengal are staring at an uncertain future as their plea to get refugee status is yet to be heard by Indian authorities.The 83 Rohingyas, including several women, were arrested in the past five-six years when they were trying to cross over to India through Bangladesh.
