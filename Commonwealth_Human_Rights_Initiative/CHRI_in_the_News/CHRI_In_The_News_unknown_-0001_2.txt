
GBA must spearhead the formation and enforcement of Paralegal ActCH
-0001
Dec 31, 2018 || News GhanaThe Commonwealth Human Rights Initiative (CHRI) Report on “Turn-around Time in Criminal Justice Administration in Ghana” has recommended that the Ghana Bar Association (GBA) leads discussions on enacting and enforcing a Paralegal Act in Ghana.The Report indicates that this would help improve access to justice delivery in the country.
