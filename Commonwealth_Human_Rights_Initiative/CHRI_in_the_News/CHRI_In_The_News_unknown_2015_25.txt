
RTI activists disappointed over CM’s ‘lack of transparency’CH
2015
Right to Information (RTI) activists who had written to Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal regarding what they claimed was a 'lack of transparency and public consultation' in relation to the proposed Delhi Lokpal Bill and the Service Delivery Amendment Bill are 'deeply disappointed' at the response they received.Mr. Kejriwal's office, the group announced on Tuesday, responded to their queries justifying the purported lack of consultation and transparency stating that the 'fundamental suggestions from the public on these crucial bills were already known, the text of the proposed legislations on above Bills has not been put in public domain for further consultations.'Read More
