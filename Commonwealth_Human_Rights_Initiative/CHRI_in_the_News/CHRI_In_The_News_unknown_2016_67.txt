
Jaipur central jail inmates to know their case through 'E-history'CH
2016
In order to facilitate the prisoners of the Jaipur central jail the Rajasthan state legal services authority and jail administration in association with commonwealth human rights initiative, an NGO started E-history facility on Sunday. The facility will enable the inmates to to know how much imprisonment is left to be served by them, when is the next day of court's presence, card of the visitors and would avail other facilities.Read More
