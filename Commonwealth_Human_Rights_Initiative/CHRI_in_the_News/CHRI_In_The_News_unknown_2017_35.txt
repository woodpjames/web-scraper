
RTI gets a boost in Kashmir's Kupwara districtCH
2017
Seeking to make Kupwara a "model RTI" district, the District Administration has invited the Jammu and Kashmir RTI Movement (J & K RTIM) and the Commonwealth Human Rights Initiative (CHRI) to provide technical assistance to public authorities for implementing the J&K Right to Information Act, 2009.For the official press release, click here:Press: ANI | Business Standard | Rising Kashmir | Kashmir Reader | Rediff
