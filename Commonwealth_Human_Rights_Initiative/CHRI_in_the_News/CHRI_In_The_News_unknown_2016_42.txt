
Theft most common crime : SurveyCH
2016
MUMBAI: Theft is the most commonly experienced crime in Mumbai and Delhi followed by sexual harassment and physical assault. In both cities, the majority of all crimes take place from 12 pm to 6 pm.Almost all the crimes surveyed are cognizable but an FIR was lodged by the police in just under half of the cases reported in the two metropolises.
