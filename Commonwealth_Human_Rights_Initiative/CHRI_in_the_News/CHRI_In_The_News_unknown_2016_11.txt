
The RTI law turns 11: A CHRI Perspective in the MediaCH
2016
October 12, 2016, marked the 11th anniversary of the Right To Information law in India. The strength of the law is impressive enough that it has been ranked among the top five in the world. That said, analysts in the Commonwealth Human Rights Initiative’s Access To Information Programme had a few thoughts on RTI use in India. The media picked them up.
