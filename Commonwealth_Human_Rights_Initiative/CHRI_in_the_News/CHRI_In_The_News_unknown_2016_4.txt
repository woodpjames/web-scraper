
Shortfalls in Police strength across seven states: Uttar Pradesh leads nation with 37 percent empty slotsCH
2016
The Commonwealth Human Rights Initiative’s analysis has found shortages in police strength across seven states in North India. Uttar Pradesh leads the country with a undermanned police force. The state has a whopping 37 percent deficiency rate in staffing cops.The CHRI analysis, featured in Amar Ujala, is here.
