Nigeria: A New MSF Emergency Project in Maiduguri

Oct 13 15
Patients describe their experiences and MSF's assistanceOn July 2, 2010, a fuel tanker crashed and exploded in Sange, South Kivu Province, Democratic Republic of Congo. Up to 269 were killed and countless injured. MSF has been providing much-needed support at Uvira Hospital. Here, survivors talk about their traumatic experiences and what MSF's assistance has meant.
