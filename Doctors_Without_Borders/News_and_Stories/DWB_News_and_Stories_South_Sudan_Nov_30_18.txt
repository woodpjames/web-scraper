South Sudan: 125 women and girls treated for sexual violence in one week 

Nov 30 18
Kasaye sits on his bed in the intensive care unit of the Doctors Without Borders/Médecins Sans Frontières (MSF) health centers in Abdurafi, Ethiopia, a bottle of soda and some cookies in his hands. He looks incredibly frail and the simple acts of drinking and eating require immense effort.“This is the thirteenth time that I have come to MSF for kala azar treatment,” he says.
