South Africa Becomes First Country to Include New, More Effective DR-TB Drug in Standard Treatment

Jun 19 18
Doctors Without Borders/Médecins Sans Frontières (MSF) staff providing treatment for childhood malnutrition in Balochistan Province, Pakistan, identified a root cause of the disease: a lack of information about how best to breastfeed. Armed with this knowledge, MSF teams are aiming to prevent malnutrition by educating mothers.
