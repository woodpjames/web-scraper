MSF Committed to Saving Lives on Mediterranean but Will Not Sign the Italian “Code of Conduct”

Aug 1 17
The United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) promise, among other things, to end malnutrition and epidemics like HIV/AIDS, TB and malaria. Unfortunately, the SDGs are not rooted in the reality and health needs faced by the people today. The SDGs lack a clear plan on how they will be achieved, and their ambitions are out of step with current trends in development policies and funding.
