Helping Victims of Torture: Khalil's Story

Jun 26 17
As people in Burundi continue to flee political unrest, Doctors Without Borders/Médecins Sans Frontières (MSF) is responding to refugees' medical needs in Nyarugusu camp, just over the Tanzania border. Among the services teams are offering is treatment for severely malnourished children, but staff are being overwhelmed by the needs. Read more.
