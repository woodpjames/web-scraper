Associated Press: Hungary Blasted for Rule Detaining Refugees in Containers

Apr 7 17
As fighting continues in the Donetsk region of eastern Ukraine, Doctors Without Borders/Médecins Sans Frontières (MSF) is providing medical supplies, including bandages, syringes, antibiotics and stretchers, to hospitals there. Teams also continue the work of treating patients with drug-resistant tuberculosis in the regional penitentiary system, a program MSF began in 2011.In addition, MSF is been running a mental health program to provide counseling to victims of violence, and technical and emotional support for mental health professionals who have been delivering psychological care.
