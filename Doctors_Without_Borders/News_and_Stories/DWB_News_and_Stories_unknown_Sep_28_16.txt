Aleppo: Two Surgical Hospitals Bombed, Only Two Others Left in Besieged City

Sep 28 16
Every year sees 60,000 more Cambodians infected with tuberculosis. The high prevalence of TB in Cambodia is pushing Doctors Without Borders/Médecins Sans Frontières (MSF) to innovate. New screening methods and treatments must be found if there is to be any chance of curbing the spread of the disease.
