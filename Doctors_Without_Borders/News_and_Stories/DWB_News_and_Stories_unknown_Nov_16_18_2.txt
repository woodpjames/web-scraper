Why I give back: "I am a doctor. I am a refugee."

Nov 16 18
In Buenaventura, Colombia, MSF psychologists receive around 1,000 calls a month through a mental health hotline—one of the few sources of psychosocial support for people in a city scarred by violence and conflict.Buenaventura is the country’s most important port, as well as a hub for drug and human trafficking. For decades, the Revolutionary Armed Forces of Colombia (FARC), right-wing paramilitary groups, and criminal gangs battled for control of the strategic region.
