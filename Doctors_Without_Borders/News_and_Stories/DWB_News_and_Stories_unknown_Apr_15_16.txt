Another group of women are cured of fistulas at the Doctors Without Borders/Médecins Sans Frontières (MSF) in Jahun, Nigeria. The program helps women who sustain injuries during obstructed childbirth and are rendered incontinent. An estimated 800,000 women are effected by fistula in Nigeria.
