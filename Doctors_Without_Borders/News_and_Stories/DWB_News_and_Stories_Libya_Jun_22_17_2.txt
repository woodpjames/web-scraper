Libya: Caring for Migrants and Refugees in the “Worst Moments” of Their Journeys

Jun 22 17
Since early May 2015, Doctors Without Borders/Médecins Sans Frontières (MSF) has been carrying out search and rescue operations in the Mediterranean Sea with two ships—the MY Phoenix (operated jointly with Migrant Offshore Aid Station) and the Bourbon Argos. MSF teams onboard work alongside search and rescue crews to provide medical assessment and care ranging from primary care to resuscitation and advanced life support.Read more stories about the people rescued on the Bourbon Argos and the MY Phoenix.
