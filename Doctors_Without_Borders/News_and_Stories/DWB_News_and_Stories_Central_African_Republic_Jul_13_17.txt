Central African Republic: Baby Shot and Killed Inside Hospital

Jul 13 17
In Tanzania's Nyarugusu camp, some 150,000 people are now crammed into an area initially planned to host some just 50,000. In response to a May cholera outbreak, Doctors Without Borders/Médecins Sans Frontières (MSF) has vaccinated 130,000 people against the disease and are distributing clean water.
