Groups call for end to Gilead’s unwarranted hepatitis C drug monopoly in Europe

Sep 11 18
Jason Cone, the executive director of Doctors Without Borders/Médecins Sans Frontières (MSF)-USA, asserts that the current US administration should stop letting pharmaceutical corporations charge any price they want for products that were created using taxpayer money. Additionally, the US government should create better incentives to encourage companies to develop desperately-needed medicines, diagnostic devices, and vaccines. View External Media.
