Novartis Secures FDA Innovation Prize for 30-Year-Old Drug

Feb 20 19
José Enrique Hernández, a nurse from one of the specialist ‘family care’ teams set up by the Ministry of Health in El Salvador’s capital city, San Salvador, has never carried out so many cervical smears, to test for cervical cancer, in a single morning session.
This was the first time that José Enrique, helped by staff from Médecins Sans Frontières (MSF), had been able to cross the invisible barrier preventing the residents of San Salvador’s La Concepción neighborhood from accessing many standard health services.
