360 Video: Treating Typhoid in DRC

Mar 31 17
"I have been bringing my children to this MSF ward for four or five years. I bring them whenever they get sick, have diarrhea or are vomiting. If I go to a different hospital they ask for money. I can’t afford it."  Doctors Without Borders/Médecins Sans Frontières (MSF) teams in eastern Balochistan provide treatment for malnutrition and run a pediatric unit for children under five. They also operate a neonatal ward in Dera Murad Jamali and outpatient therapeutic feeding programs in the area. Read more patient stories from eastern Balochistan here.
