No Health Without Mental Health

Oct 10 17
A look at some of the Doctors Without Borders/Médecins Sans Frontières (MSF) activities this month: mental health care in the West Bank; the outpouring of volunteer assistance to refugees in Calais and Dunkirk, France; maternal and neonatal care in Kabul, Afghanistan; treating malaria in South Sudan; and continuing to provide medical care and assistance to refugees arriving in Europe as the winter begins.
