Arabic The present document outlines the strategy of the EuroMed Rights for the period of 2012-2018. The document aims at communicating effectively EuroMed Rights’ objectives and methodologies within its membership as well as to the outside world. It seeks to develop new synergies, partnerships and internal communication by offering a shared vision of the network, its values and objectives. The document is divided into five parts: The strategy provides the overall direction for EuroMed Rights’ work. It will be translated into programmes outlining in greater detail the chosen interventions implementing the strategy.
