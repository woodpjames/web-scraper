French 
Arabic This report describes the main elements of EuroMed Rights’ activities since its last General Assembly in Brussels in June 2015. It mainly addresses the members of the Network, but also interested observers from civil society, public institutions and donors. The purpose is to give a narrative account to EuroMed Rights’ members about the implementation by EuroMed Rights of the Work Programme that was adopted at the last General Assembly. Hence, after describing the basic work methods of the Network, the report goes through various parts of the past three years’ Work Programme, recalls the commitments made at the 2015 General Assembly and points out our achievements, results and reflections.
