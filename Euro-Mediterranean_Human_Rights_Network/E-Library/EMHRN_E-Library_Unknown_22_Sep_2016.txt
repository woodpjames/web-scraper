22 Sep 2016
Alerts,
Migration and Asylum September 22, 2016 The International community cannot merely “consider facilitating” mobility: weak commitments on international protection (resettlement target withdrawn from Declaration!) and safe and legal pathways contrasts sharply with clear maintenance of border control and (forced) return cooperation. Consultation with civil society is not “desirable” but a pre-requisite for genuine international and human rights based efforts.
