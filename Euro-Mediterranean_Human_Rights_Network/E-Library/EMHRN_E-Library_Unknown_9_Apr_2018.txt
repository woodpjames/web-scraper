Letter to UN Security Council: Human rights at the heart of the extension of the MINURSO’s mandate
9 Apr 2018
Morocco / Western Sahara,
Statement April 09, 2018 Read in: 
French On 27 April, Member States of the United Nations Security Council will give their decision on the renewal of the UN mission for a referendum in Western Sahara. This is an opportunity for EuroMed Rights to call out the situation of human rights in Western Sahara. In a letter to Heads of State, we reminded them of the importance of a just and sustainable solution in the region, in conformity with the principles of the Charter of the United Nations and the previous resolutions of the Security Council.
