French The following Action Plan looks forward into the period ranging from 2004 to 2008. It describes the values upon which the  EMHRN builds its activities and the human rights and socio-political environment of the Barcelona process. Finally, it outlines the main tracks of work and methodologies of the EMHRN in the next years to come. The Plan of Action should be adopted by the AGM in Malta 5-7 December. Thereafter, under the responsibility of the  Executive Committee, the EMHRN will draft a detailed program of activities aimed at implementing the Action Plan according  to the Malta Assembly’s recommendations.
