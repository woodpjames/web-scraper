TUNISIA : Civil society presents a draft law against discriminations
30 Jun 2016
Justice and the Rule of Law,
Tunisia June 30, 2016 Read in: 
French 
Arabic The Tunisian forum on economic and social rightsthe, the Committee for the Respect of Liberties and Human Rights and Euromed Rights prepared a draft law against all forms of discrimination, composed by 36 articles.
The draft law aims at establishing a legal framework to protect the victims of racism in accordance with the international conventions. Any discrimination practice based on religion, nationality and skin colour will be considered as a crime…
