Arabic During a national conference to present its publication “Violence à l’égard des femmes: les lois du genre” (Violence against women: gender law), prepared by Sana Ben Achour, EuroMed Rights points out that  discriminatory laws facilitate violence against women and girls in the first place.
This publication provides an overview of the challenges for combating violence against women, but also it analyses the law that should be changed to eradicated this phenomenon. Finally, an advocacy plan for a law against gender violence has been developed. Read the full publication
