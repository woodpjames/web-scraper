Islamist Groups Must Stop Inciting to Sectarian Violence; Protection of Christians Responsibility of the State
7 Aug 2013
Egypt,
Press Release August 07, 2013 Read in: 
Arabic The undersigned organizations express their grave concern regarding the increasing sectarian violence which has targeted Christians and their churches since the uprising of June 30.  These organizations strongly condemn the rhetoric employed by leaders of the Muslim Brotherhood and their allies which includes clear incitement to violence and religious hatred in order to achieve political gains, regardless of the grave repercussions of such rhetoric for peace in Egypt.
