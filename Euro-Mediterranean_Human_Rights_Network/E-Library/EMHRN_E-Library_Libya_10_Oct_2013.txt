EMHRN statement on the release of Libyan Prime Minister Ali Zeidan
10 Oct 2013
Justice and the Rule of Law,
Libya,
Statement October 10, 2013 Read in: 
French 
Arabic Following the release of Libyan Prime Minister, Ali Zeidan, the Euro-Mediterranean Human Rights Network (EMHRN) expresses its grave concern regarding his abduction. Such act can never be justified and constitutes a serious obstacle to the establishment of the rule of law. EMHRN welcomes the release of Ali Zeidan and calls for the assurance that the perpetrators of his abduction are brought before Justice.
