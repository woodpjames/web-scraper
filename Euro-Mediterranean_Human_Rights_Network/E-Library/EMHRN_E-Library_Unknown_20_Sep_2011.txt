Arabic Read the Policy Paper here The general objective of the EMHRN Gender Equality Policy is to promote gender equality in order to contribute to the transformation of the societies in the Euro-Mediterranean region. To do that, it is a prerequisite that the EMHRN is able to achieve gender equality in its structures and activities.
