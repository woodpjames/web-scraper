ISRAEL: EuroMed Rights Condemns Hunger Strikers Force-feeding Law
30 Jul 2015
Israel / OPT,
Press Release July 30, 2015 Today, the Israeli Knesset legalised force-feeding of hunger strikers in detention. EuroMed Rights has repeatedly called on the EU to condemn force-feeding of hunger strikers, considered a form of cruel, inhuman and degrading treatment. See previous EuroMed Rights statement on this issue here.
