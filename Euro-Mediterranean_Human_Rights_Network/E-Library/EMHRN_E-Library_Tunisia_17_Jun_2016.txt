EVENT: “Tripartite dialogue” as a key tool for civil society, Tunisia and the EU
17 Jun 2016
Campaign,
Economic & Social Rights,
Europe,
Tunisia June 17, 2016 On Thursday 16 June, EuroMed Rights organised a conference at the European Commission, with Commissioner for International Trade and Trade Agreements Cecilia Malmström, Director General for Neighbourhood and Enlargement negotiations Christian Danielsson, Tunisian Minister Kamel Jendoubi and MEP Pier Antonio Panzeri to discuss the concept of a “Tripartite dialogue” with ‎Tunisia‬ as a key tool for civil society to monitor bilateral relations between the EU and third countries.
