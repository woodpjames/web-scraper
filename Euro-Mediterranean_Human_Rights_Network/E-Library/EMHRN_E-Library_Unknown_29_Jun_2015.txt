Syria: Joint Oral Intervention at the Human Rights Council
29 Jun 2015
Press Release,
Syria June 29, 2015 Read in: 
French 
Arabic On 23 June, EuroMed Rights and the Cairo Institute for Human Rights Studies delivered an oral intervention during the interactive dialogue with the International Independent Commission of Inquiry on Syria, in the framework of the 29th session of the Human Rights Council. The intervention, delivered by Syrian lawyer Anouar Al Bunni, emphasized the constant failure of the UN Security Council to address the prevailing climate of impunity in Syria and urged UN member states to explore alternative paths of accountability in Syria, including the use of universal jurisdiction to prosecute war criminals.
