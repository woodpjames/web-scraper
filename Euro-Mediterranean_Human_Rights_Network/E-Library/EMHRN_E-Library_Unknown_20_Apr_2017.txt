Letter to Member States of the UNSC on the extension of the MINURSO’s mandate
20 Apr 2017
Economic & Social Rights,
Morocco / Western Sahara,
Open Letter April 20, 2017 On 27 April, the Member States of the United Nations Security Council will take a decision on the renewal of the mandate of the United Nations Mission for the Referendum in Western Sahara (MINURSO). On this occasion, EuroMed Rights calls on the Heads of State and Government to ensure that human rights are at the heart of these discussions Human rights must be at the heart of the extension of the MINURSO’s mandate.
