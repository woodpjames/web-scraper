ALERT / EGYPT: Human rights lawyer Malek Adly arrested
6 May 2016
Alerts,
Egypt,
Justice and the Rule of Law May 06, 2016 Read in: 
Arabic Brussels, 6 May 2016 – Human rights defender and lawyer Malek Adly and political activist Zizo Abdo were arrested in Cairo Thursday night. Malek Adly was questioned by a prosecutor and is now detained for 15 days pending investigations on charges of inciting protests and calling to overthrow the regime. He has denied all charges held against him.
