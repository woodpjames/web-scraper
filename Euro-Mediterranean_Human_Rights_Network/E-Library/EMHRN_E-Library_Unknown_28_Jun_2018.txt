80 Human Rights Organisations from the Mediterranean Region Gathered in Brussels for the 11th EuroMed Rights General Assembly
28 Jun 2018
Statement June 28, 2018 Read in: 
French 
Arabic  On 23 and 24 June 2018, more than a hundred representatives from 80 organisations working on Human Rights on the two shores of the Mediterranean gathered in Brussels to attend the 11th General Assembly of EuroMed Rights.
