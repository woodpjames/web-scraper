The Prohibition Of Torture Within The Context Of Emergency Situations
February 19 2008
On 04 March, the APT, the American University Washington College of Law and the Inter-American Commission on Human Rights cosponsored a program on 'The Prohibition Of Torture Within The Context of Emergency Situations'. For full details of the event, including panelists, please consult the Program Flyer. The event took place in Washington DC.
