Panama commits to create torture prevention body
May 13 2015
Last week, Panama appeared before the UN Human Rights Council in Geneva for its second Universal Periodic Review (UPR).During the review, the Council member States evaluated the status of implementation of recommendations made five years ago and the human rights situation, including in the prevention of torture.
