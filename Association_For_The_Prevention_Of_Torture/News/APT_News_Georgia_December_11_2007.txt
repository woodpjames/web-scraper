Georgia's OPCAT Implementation
December 11 2007
Penal Reform International's Tbilisi Regional OfficeGeorgian Branch of the Global Initiative on PsychiatryThe meeting was designed to take stock of the steps taken by the government over the past six months with regard to the OPCAT implementation. Regrettably, little headway apperas to have been made in this respect, as the Georgian authorities have yet to decide on an appropriate institution.The round-table was part of a three-year European Union-funded project to promote independent detention monitoring in Georgia. The partners will continue to monitor the implementation of the OPCAT.
