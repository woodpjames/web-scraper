First South America OPCAT implementation seminar
May 24 2007
The APT organized, in Asuncion, Paraguay, on 29 May, the first regional seminar for MERCOSUR countries of South America on the national implementation of the Optional Protocol. The seminar enabled a review of national actions between, for the first time, three Latin American members of the new international Subcommittee for the Prevention of Torture  with Government Officials and NGOs from Argentina, Bolivia, Brazil, Chile, Paraguay, Peru, Uruguay and other observer States.Boletin de PrensaEvent Program, Draft Conclusions, Boletin de PrensaActa Final, Relatorio Seminario Protocolo TorturaClaudia Gerez, APT Americas Programme Officer
