Proceedings of Central America OPCAT Seminar Available (in Spanish)
January 13 2009
The 1st Regional Conference on OPCAT in Central America and Mexico took place in Tegucigalpa, Honduras from 15 – 16 October 2008 bringing together international and local authorities, experts and practitioners.The Proceedings of the Conference – organized by the CPTRT (Honduras), ODHAG (Guatemala) and RCT (Denmark) and sponsored by the IIDH (Costa Rica) and the APT – highlight some of the issues discussed, including:The Declaration of Tegucigalpa on the OPCAT was adopted at the end of the Seminar.
