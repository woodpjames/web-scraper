The Prevention of Torture in Practice: Seminar on the implementation in Brazil of the OPCAT
February 15 2007
This seminar, held from 23-25 April in Brazil, had the objectives of disseminating knowledge among relevant national actors about the OPCAT, particularly its provisions regarding the national preventive mechanisms (NPMs) and the bringing together of such actors to advance the process of establishing the Brazilian NPM.Claudia Gerez, APT Americas Programme Officercgerez@apt.ch
