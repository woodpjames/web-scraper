Protection of Detention Monitors (2007-2008)The APT will be developing a project during 2007 to explore protection strategies for persons monitoring places of detention and for people deprived of their liberty, threatened as a result of this work.    Truth Commissions and Torture Prevention (2002-2003)The APT jointly published with the Chilean NGO CODEPU (Corporación de Defensa y Promoción de los Derechos del Pueblo) a study entitled 'Truth Commissions: an uncertain path? Comparative study on Truth Commission in Argentina, Chile, El Salvador, Guatemala and South Africa, from the perspective of victims and human rights organizations' (Link to study in Spanish and to summary in English)
