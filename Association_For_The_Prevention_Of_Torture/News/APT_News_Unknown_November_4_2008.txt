Combating Torture: A Manual for Judges and Prosecutors
November 4 2008
As a result of the event a group of around 30 judges, prosecutors, defence lawyers and Ombudsman's Office employees learned more about their duties and responsibilities to prevent and investigate acts of torture and other forms of ill-treatment. In addition to enhancing the knowledge of the participants in the area of torture prevention, the event was designed to encourage greater use of the University of Essex's invaluable manual in Georgia and an increased awareness of the important lessons contained therein.'Combating Torture: A Manual for Judges and Prosecutors' - Georgian Version, University of EssexAgenda
