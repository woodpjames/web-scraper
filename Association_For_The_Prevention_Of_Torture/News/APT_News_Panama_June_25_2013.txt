Human Rights Rapporteur urges Panama to establish its NPM
June 25 2013
The Rapporteur on persons deprived of their liberty of the Inter-American Commission on Human Rights, Commissioner Rodrigo Escobar Gil, was invited by the APT on 14 June to present the regional report on human rights of persons deprived of their liberty in the Americas.
