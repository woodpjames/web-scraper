Loss of Patricio Rice
July 9 2010
The APT is very sad to have learned of the sudden death of Patricio Rice, The Argentinian / Irish human rights activist, Coordinator of the International Coalition Against Enforced Disappearances, co-founder of FEDEFAM, member of CELS and survivor of torture during the military dictatorship.The APT extends its sincere condolences to his family and very extensive network of friends.
