Derman v Turkey
November 29 2010
On 31 May 2011 in the case of Derman v Turkey the Court found the latter in violation of Article 3 for failing to punish three police officers found guilty of torturing the complainant in 1999. By failing to punish the officers during their trial in 2001 the domestic authorities had effectively allowed physical and psychological suffering go unpunished.
