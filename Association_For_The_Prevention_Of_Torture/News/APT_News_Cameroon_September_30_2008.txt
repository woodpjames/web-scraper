Cameroon signs the Optional Protocol to the UN Convention against Torture
September 30 2008
Cameroon signed the Optional Protocol to the UN Convention against Torture on 15 December. There are now 50 States Parties and 24 Signatories* to the OPCAT. For a complete overview please consult our up-to-date list of Ratifications and Signatures.*Signatories: Not taken into account are States that have already ratified or acceeded to the OPCAT.
