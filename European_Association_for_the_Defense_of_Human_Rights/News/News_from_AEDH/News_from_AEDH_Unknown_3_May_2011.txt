AEDH comments the CJEU El Dridi ruling – 28 April 2011
3 May 2011
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) The Court of Justice of the European Union (CJEU) decided, on April 28th 2011, that prison term does not respect the 2008 “Return” European directive when irregular stay is the only motive. This decision is certainly a way to counter criminalizing policies towards immigrants but must not lead to consider the “Return” directive as a positive and useful tool of the EU migration policy.
