The future of the Stockholm Programme: AEDH claims a Europe of fundamental rights for all! – Answer to the Commission’s consultation – 21 January 2014
21 January 2014
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) The Stockholm Programme is coming to an end in 2014. Designed for the period 2010-2014, the third plan of the European Union concerning the area of freedom, security and justice followed the Tampere Programme (2000-2004) and the Hague Programme (2005-2009). AEDH seizes the opportunity to express its point of view on this issue and on the European policies in the field of justice and home affairs (JHA), in the framework of the public consultation launched by the European Commission. The AEDH answer to the public consultation is available in French.
