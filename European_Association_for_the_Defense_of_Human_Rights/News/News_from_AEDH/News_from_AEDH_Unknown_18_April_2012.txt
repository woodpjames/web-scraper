After this seminar, the AEDH will organise its General assembly, also in Brno, on 28 and 29 April 2012. During this General assembly, the numerous member leagues of the AEDH as well as its individual members will gather to discuss the upcoming actions of the association and to adopt its activity report of 2011-2012. They will also vote for a new chairman and for new members of the executive board.
