Human rights days marking the 25th anniversary of Associacion Pro Derechos Humanos de Andalucia
30 November 2015
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) AEDH will participate in the Human Rights Days marking the 25th Anniversary of the Asociacion Pro Derechos Humanos de Andalucia from 4th to 6th December 2015 in Seville For 25 years, the APDHA has been working to make visible and denounce violations of human rights in Andalusia, in Spain and in the rest of the world.  On the occasion of its 25th anniversary, the APDHA invites national and international experts, associations, platforms and entities defense of human rights to speak on emerging issues concerning human rights, 67 years after the Universal Declaration of Human Rights.  For APDHA, this event is also an opportunity to strengthen the links between the different organizations that are fighting for the defense of human rights.  AEDH’s president, Dominique Guibert, will speak on the theme "The defense of human rights at an international level from the organized civil society ", on Sunday 6th December.
