3 November 2009 – The AEDH has signed the civil society Declaration for “Global privacy standards for a Global World”
3 November 2009
The AEDH has signed the civil society Declaration for “Global privacy standards for a Global world”. On November, 3rd, the AEDH will attend the conference organized by the Public Voice coalition in Madrid, on this topic. Download the Civil society Declaration in English, Spanish, Catalan, Portuguese and French.
