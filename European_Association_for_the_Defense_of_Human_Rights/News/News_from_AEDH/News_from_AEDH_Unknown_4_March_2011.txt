    Français (French) The AEDH signed, together with a great number of NGOs, the Call on the European Union for a support intervention in the Mediterranean area. While major changes bringing an end to autoritarian regimes began ain the Mediterranean area, governments and institutions from the EU are first concerned with the control of “uncontrollable migration flows”.
