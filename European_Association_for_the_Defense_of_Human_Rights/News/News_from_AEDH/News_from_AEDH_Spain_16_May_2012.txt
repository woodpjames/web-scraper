Joint civil society declaration on access to health care services for undocumented migrants in Spain
16 May 2012
AEDH signed a joint declaration of European and Spanish NGOs and professional organisations, on access to healthcare for irregular migrants in Spain. This joint civil society declaration was sent on the 15th of May 2012 to all members of the Spanish Parliament, ahead of the vote on a royal decree that among other measures restricts health care access for irregular migrants.
