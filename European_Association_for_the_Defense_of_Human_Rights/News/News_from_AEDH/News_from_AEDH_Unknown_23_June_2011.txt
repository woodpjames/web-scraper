Intervention of the AEDH President at the LDH Congress – 11 June 2011
23 June 2011
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) Pierre Barge, President of AEDH, attended the Congress of the French Human Rights League on June 11th, 12th and 13th 2011 which took place in Reims (France). In his intervention, he pointed out the state of play of AEDH works and what is currently at stake at the European level. Download his intervention (only available in French).
