Open Letter to EU institutions and agencies: “Protect Syrian refugees!”
20 March 2014
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) On 6 March 2014, over 100 organisations from over 30 countries, including AEDH, launched a campaign "Europe, act now !"  to call on European governments and the EU institutions to make a concerted European effort to offer protection to those fleeing the conflict in Syria in a spirit of solidarity with Syria’s neighbouring countries. In this framework, AEDH cosigns an open letter asking European leaders to act now to ensure access to protection for the men, women and children fleeing the conflict in Syria. Read the Open Letter
