Mass surveillance and citizens
31 January 2018
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) AEDH and ELSA organize a film-debate on the film “Nothing to hide” on Tuesday, February 6, 2018 from 18:45, at Saint-Louis University (43 bd Botanical Garden, 1000 Brussels, audience number 7). Sunday, January 28, 2018 took place the International Data Protection Day. In order to highlight the importance of this theme and in order to raise awareness on this issue, which affects all citizens, AEDH organizes the broadcasting of the film “Nothing to Hide” followed by a debate led by our expert in protection data, Maryse Artiguelong. “Nothing to hide” is a Franco-German documentary directed by Marc Meillassoux and Mihaela Gladovic, which focuses on the effects of surveillance on individuals and society and its degree of acceptance by the population.
