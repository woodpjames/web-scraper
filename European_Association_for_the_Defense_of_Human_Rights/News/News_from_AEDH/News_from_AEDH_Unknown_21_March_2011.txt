AEDH reponse to the Commission’s consultation on a general approach to the protection of personal data in the EU – 15 January 2011
21 March 2011
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) The AEDH welcomes the fact that the European Commission carries out a consultation on the modification of the 1995 Directive on data protection. The AEDH indeed thinks that it’s time to amend that Directive as to conform it with technologic changes that happened since 1995 and to comply with European and national developments in case law.
