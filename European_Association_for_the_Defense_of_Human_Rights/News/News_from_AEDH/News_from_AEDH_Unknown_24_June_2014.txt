The European Association for the Defence of Human Rights held its General Assembly on June 21st and 22nd 2014
24 June 2014
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) Gathered for the General Assembly on June 21st and 22nd, the members of AEDH have renewed the executive board of the association for a period of two years. Following a first meeting on June 22nd resulting in the vote of the positions of members, the new board is composed as follows: Dominique Guibert, Ligue des droits de l’Homme, France, chairman  Philippos Mittleton, Hellenic League for Human Rights, Greece, vice-chairman Catherine Teule, Ligue des droits de l’Homme, France, vice-chairwoman Gerard van Vliet, Liga voor de Rechten van de Mens, Pays-Bas, vice-chairman  Knut Albrecht, Internationale Liga für Menschenrechte, Germany, secretary general Rafael Lara, Asociación Pro Derechos Humanos Andalucía, Spain, secretary general Mohamed Zyad, Liga voor de Rechten van de Mens, Netherlands, secretary general  David Busuttil, Malta Association of Human Rights (MAHR), Malta, treasurer  Maryse Artiguelong, Ligue des droits de l’Homme (LDH), France, board member Maria Vittoria Arpaia, Lega Italiana Dei Diritti Dell’Uomo (LIDU), Italia, board member Maria Stefanie Cataleta, Lega Italiana Dei Diritti Dell’Uomo (LIDU), Italia, board member Rumiana Decheva, European Association for the Defence of Human Rights- Bulgaria, board member Stefanie Gryson, Liga voor Mensenrechten, Belgium, board member Manuel Malheiros, Liga Portuguesa dos Direitos Humanos – Civitas, Portugal, board member Tanveer Parnez, Black and Ethnic minorities in Scotland (BEMIS), board member Emilie Pesselier, board member, representative of individual members Marija Staciokiene, Lithuanian Human Rights League, Lithuania, board member Stoil Tzitzelkov, board member, representative of individual members Tony van Loon, Liga voor Mensenrechten, Belgium, board member Roberto Vismara, Lega Italiana Dei Diritti Dell’Uomo (LIDU), Italia, board member Angelika Watzl, Österreichische Liga für Menschenrechte, Austria, board member  Furthermore, the General Assembly adopted the resolution No Europe without rights! (see http://www.aedh.eu/No-Europe-without-rights.html ).
