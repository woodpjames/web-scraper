Liga Voor Mensenrechten & Ligue wallonne des Droits de l’Homme – Launching the website “Retain your privacy”: Campaign against the Belgian transposition of the European Directive on the retention of data – October, 26th 2009
28 October 2009
A wide platform of organisations, including the Flemish League for Human Rights and the Walloon League for Human Rights, has launched a campaign against the Belgian transposition of the European data retention directive. Please find below the links to the campaign website and the petition: http://bewaarjeprivacy.be/nl (for the Dutch version) http://preservetavieprivee.be (for the French version) Please find enclosed the press release in its English, Dutch and French versions.
