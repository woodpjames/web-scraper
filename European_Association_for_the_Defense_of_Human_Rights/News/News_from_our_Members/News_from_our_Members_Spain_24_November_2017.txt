APDHA, Spain – Migreurop member organisations in Spain demand the Spanish government to respect human rights in border management
24 November 2017
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) The Spanish Migreurop member organisations urge the government to take its responsibility regarding the media treatment of the situation. Images and speeches criminalising victims lead to problems of cohabitation and an increase of Islamophobia, racism and discrimination. They also demand the closure of the Archidona Detention Center, the end of immediate expulsions, and more urgently the compliance with international human rights law in border management.
