Member News: The Luxembourg’s League of Human Rights questions political parties
9 October 2013
On the eve of the elections of October 20th, the Luxembourg’s League of Human Rights (Action Luxembourg Ouvert et Solidaire) sent a questionnaire to the competing political parties to ask them to take a position regarding the key issues related to the defense of Human Rights and Democracy. Their answers as well as a comparative table are available in French on the website of the League.
