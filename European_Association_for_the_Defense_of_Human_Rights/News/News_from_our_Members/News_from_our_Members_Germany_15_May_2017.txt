Internationale Liga für Menschenrechte (ILMR), Germany – The right to conscientious objection must be a universal and unconditional Human Right!
15 May 2017
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) At the occasion of the International Day on Conscientious Objection and in the event of the repeated demand in the last few months for the resurrection of compulsory military service, our German member, the International League for Human Rights (ILMR) calls for the creation and implementation of a universal and unconditional Human Right to conscientious objection.
