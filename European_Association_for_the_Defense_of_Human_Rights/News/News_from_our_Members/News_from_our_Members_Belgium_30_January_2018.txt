Liga voor mensenrechten (Liga), Belgium. Housing Search Act: Civil Society Requires More Guarantees for Human Rights
30 January 2018
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) The parliamentary committee of internal affairs decided on 23rd January not to vote on the draft law authorizing house searches of homeless persons who do not voluntarily obey an order to leave the territory. The vote was postponed because of strong criticism from many citizens, organisations and experts. The Liga welcomes the report of the vote and requests the bill’s amendment. Read more (NL)
