Helsinki committee wins lawsuit against cabinet office over Stop Soros national consultation
28 February 2018
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) 28 February 2018 – The Metropolitan Court of Budapest in its first instance ruling found that in the ‘National Consultation’ questionnaire, the Cabinet Office of the Prime Minister had violated the Hungarian Helsinki Committee’s right to good reputation. The court found that the statements in Question 5 of the questionnaire related to the Hungarian Helsinki Committee (HHC) are false and misrepresent the human rights organization. The Court called on the government to issue an apology as well as pay the HHC HUF 2 million for damages. The ruling is subject to appeal. https://www.helsinki.hu/en/hhc-wins-lawsuit-against-cabinet/
