The Belgian League of Human Rights (LDH) – #jenelabouclepas : Crowdfunding campaign to support citizen solidarity
11 October 2017
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) The LDH has launched a fundraising campaign to help six passengers charged with preventing the violent deportation of an undocumented immigrant. The events took place on 17 August 2016 on a commercial flight to Cameroon. The six defendants must appear in court on 15 November 2017. To ensure their defence, the LDH seeks to collect € 500 per defendant (or € 3.000 total). Read more (FR)
