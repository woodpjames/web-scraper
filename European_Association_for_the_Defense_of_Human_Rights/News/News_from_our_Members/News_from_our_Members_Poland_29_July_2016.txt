Open Republic denounces the increased number of hate speeches in Poland
29 July 2016
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) 7 July 2016 – Magdalena Czyz, Vice-president of Open Republic (OTWARTA RZECZPOSPOLITA) expresses its concerns about the increased number of hate speeches in Poland, which tend to become increasingly common without any real awareness or measures taken by the police or the Ministry of Justice. She also encourages the use of Open Republic’s internet platform called " incidents database" in order to report manifestations of hate speeches and hate acts in the itnernet and in public space.
