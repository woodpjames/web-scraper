Unione forense per la tutela dei diritti umani, LIDU and APDHA denounce the Italian Minister Salvini’s refusal to welcome the Aquarius boat
29 May 2018
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) 29 May 2018 – Unione forense per la tutela dei diritti umani (IT), LIDU (IT) and APDHA (ES) denounce the decision of the Italian Deputy Prime Minister, Matteo Salvini, who ordered the closure of the Italian ports, refusing access to the NGO SOS Méditerranée‘s boat Aquarius with 629 migrants on board.
