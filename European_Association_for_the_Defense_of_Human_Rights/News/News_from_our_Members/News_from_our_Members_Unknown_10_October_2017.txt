10 October 2017

    Français (French) IDHC offers a fellowship to human rights defenders from the American continent, Eastern Europe, Maghreb and the Middle East who wish to participate in the annual Human Rights Course organised by the institute itself. The course will take place from 9 to 26 April 2018. The fellowship covers travel and living expenses for the duration of the course. Read more (ES)
