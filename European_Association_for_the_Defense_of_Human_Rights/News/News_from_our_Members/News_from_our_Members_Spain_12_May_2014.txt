Spain : APDHA publishes its annual report on immigration “Human Rights at the southern border” – May 2014
12 May 2014
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) Because of the current context, the APHA 2014 report focuses on Ceuta and Melilla recent events. By doing so, APDHA wants to underline that these two “scenarios” are no isolated cases but the result of two decades of disastrous external border management policies. APDHA 2014 report is available in Spanish, English and French
