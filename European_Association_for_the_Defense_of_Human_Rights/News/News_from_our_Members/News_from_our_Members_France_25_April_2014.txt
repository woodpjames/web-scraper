France: Stop the deportation of aliens waiting for medical care! – April 2014
25 April 2014
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) In France, a seriously ill alien remains first and foremost a stranger. The process is always the same, foreigners suffer from inhuman treatments: denial of rights, arrest, detention and deportation to countries that offer no guarantee of access to care. In order to denounce this fact, the French League of Human Rights (LDH) co-signed, on April 17, an article published in the French newspaper Le Monde.
