Otwarta Rzeczpospolita, Poland – Joint statement of 85 civil society organisations following the death of a 14-year-old boy
23 September 2017
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) 85 Polish associations have come together to denounce the discrimination and violence suffered by children and teenagers in schools. So far, the government has done nothing to protect the victims of school harassment. On the contrary, some politicians fuel these discriminations, especially against foreigners and homosexuals, using hate speech. Read more (PL)
