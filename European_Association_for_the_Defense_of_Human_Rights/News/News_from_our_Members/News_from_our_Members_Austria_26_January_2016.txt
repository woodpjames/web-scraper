Liga für Menschenrechte publishes its report on the situation of human rights in 2015 in Austria
26 January 2016
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) 30 December 2016 – The Austrian League for the Defence of Human Rights condemns the discrimination persons with disabilities and LGBTI people had to face in 2015. It also denounces the flaws of the asylum system in Austria and the violations of human rights that result from it.  The league also recommends to further integrate human rights issues in the Austrian development policy agenda and regrets the limited resources that are made available in this regard.  Finally, it puts into question the responsibility of Austrian companies and their actions in the Southern Hemisphere. Read more
