Ligue des droits de l’Homme,  Belgium –  Concerns about  the growing number of Muslim headscarf bans
15 July 2017
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) July 15 2017 – This note, written by our member, the Belgian French-speaking league of Human Rights, analyses from a fundamental rights perspective, bans on headscarves targeting adult women. It concludes that the prohibition on headscarves turns out to be compatible with the right to freedom of religion and the right to non-discrimination only in very specific limited situations.
