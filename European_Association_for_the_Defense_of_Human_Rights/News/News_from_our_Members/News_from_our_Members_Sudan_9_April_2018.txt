The Swiss League for Human Rights – contests and calls for the cancellation of the special flight to Sudan from Switzerland
9 April 2018
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) 9 April 2018 – The Swiss League for Human Rights contests and calls for the cancellation of the special flight to Sudan from Switzerland due to the risk of internment and torture for the returnees. Amnesty International’s report 2017/18 denounces the “catastrophic” security and humanitarian situation in Darfur and the Blue Nile and South Kordofan states, and warns against “arbitrary arrests, detention and other human rights violations perpetrated by security forces” and the restriction of “rights to freedom of expression, association and peaceful assembly”.
