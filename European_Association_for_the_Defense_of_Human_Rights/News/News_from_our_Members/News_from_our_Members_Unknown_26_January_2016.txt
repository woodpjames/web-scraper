Asociación Pro Derechos Humanos de Andalucia (APDHA) asks for the release of the Seville firefighters detained in Lesbos
26 January 2016
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) 15 January 2016 – APDHA denounces the arbitrary detention of three Sevillian firefighters and two Danish volunteers by the Greek authorities who accuse them of being involved in human trafficking. The three firefighters had co-founded an association to help the refugees and are now held as criminals.  APDHA condemns the cruelty of European policies at the EU’s border and expresses its solidarity with the detainees. Read more
