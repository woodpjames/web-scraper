HLHR (Greece): Statement of the Hellenic league – 16 April 2012
4 May 2012
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) The Hellenic league of human rights published on 16 April, 2012, a Statement on various violations of human rights in Greece. It asks the government to put an end to many situations that violate human rights in the country, such as the detention of immigrants, torture in prison, racism, and so on. This Statement was writen in the aim to contribute to the work of the Committee against Torture of the United Nations. Download the Statement.
