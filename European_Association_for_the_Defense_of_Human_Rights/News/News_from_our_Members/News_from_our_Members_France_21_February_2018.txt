LDH – France – The Asylum-Immigration Law: an unbalanced project that worries associations
21 February 2018
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) 21 February 2018 – LDH-France and 20 other civil society organisations are calling on parliamentarians to make major changes to the asylum-immigration law presented by the Government in the Council of Ministers. While some measures are positive, the text mainly contains measures that will degrade the asylum procedure and the social support proposed by the associations, in a context where all the asylum seekers cannot be accommodated, due to an insufficient number of places.
