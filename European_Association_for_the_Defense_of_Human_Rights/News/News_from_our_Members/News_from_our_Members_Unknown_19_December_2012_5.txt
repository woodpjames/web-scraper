the League for Human Rights publishes its third letter “Human rights in central and oriental Europe” – November 2012
19 December 2012
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) The publication of this letter aims at deepening and make people know about the political, social and economic evolutions that happen in Europe, particularly in the countries of Eastern Europe, but not only. Through this letter, the LDH wants to give a reflexion about the news of societies and organs that fight for human rights and thus, to lead to debates and solidarity actions.
