Otwarta Rzeczpospolita,  Poland –  Open letter to the Polish Minister of Education for a anti-discrimination education
7 July 2017

    Français (French) July 7 2017 – Our Polish member Otwarta Rzeczpospolita wrote with more than 82 non-governmental organisations an open letter to the Polish Minister of Education, Anna Zalewska, to ensure an anti-discrimination education, free from prejudice, hatred and intolerance in all educational institutions.
