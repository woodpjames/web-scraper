The Hungarian Helsinki Committee – Independent civil society attacked in Hungary
11 September 2017
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) In a joint statement, our member association, the Hungarian Helsinki Committee, wanted to draw the attention of the Organisation for Security and Co-operation in Europe (OSCE) to alarming shrinking civic space for civil society. Under the Law on the Transparency of Organisations Receiving Foreign Funds (Anti-NGO Law), adopted on 13 June 2017, any civil society organisation that receives more than USD 28 000 per year from foreign sources must register as an “organisation receiving foreign funds” in a state register and display this stigmatising label on all of its publications. Hungarian Helsinki Committee and other Hungarian NGOs call for the repeal of this law. Read more (EN)
