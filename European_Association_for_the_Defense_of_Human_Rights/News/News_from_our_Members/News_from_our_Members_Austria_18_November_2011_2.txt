The Austrian Human Rights League coordinates the drafting of the report of the Austrian NGO platform on Human rights – June 2010
18 November 2011
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) The Austrian Human Rights League coordinated the drafting of the report of the Austrian NGO platform on Human rights which brings together 270 NGOs from the country. For more information about the NGO network, click here. This report will be presented at the occasion of the Government’s meeting "Universal Periodic Review" in January 2011 and aims at involving the Austrian civil society in the elaboration of Human rights policies of the country.
