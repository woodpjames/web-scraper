Malta Association of Human Rights (MAHR) condemns the murder of Daphne Caruana Galizia
16 October 2017
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) 16 October 2017 – MAHR condemns the brutal murder of investigative journalist Daphne Caruana Galizia. Her assassination is an attack on the very core of the Democratic Republic of Malta. MAHR calls on the Maltese government, parliament and its institutions to do justice. Malta cannot remain silent faced with those who threaten its fundamental rights and values. Read the press release.
