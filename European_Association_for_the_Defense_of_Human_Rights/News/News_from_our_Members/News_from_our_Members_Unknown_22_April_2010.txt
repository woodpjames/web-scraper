APDHA (Andalucia): Petition – European citizenship and voting rights for all residents
22 April 2010
AEDH is pleased to inform you about the latest campaign appeal launched by APDHA (Asociación Pro Derechos Humanos de Andalucía). The APDHA plans to petition the Spanish Presidency to support voting rights for all residents, not only citizens, within the EU and to urge the Spanish Prime Minister Zapatero to present this initiative to the European Council, helping to ensure a more democratic and inclusive Europe. You can add your signature to the petition by visiting their website. The appeals is available in English, French, Spanish or Italian.
