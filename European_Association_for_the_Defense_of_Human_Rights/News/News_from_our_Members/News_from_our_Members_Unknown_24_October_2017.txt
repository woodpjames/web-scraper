The French Human Rights League (LDH) – Open letter to the President of the French Republic concerning unaccompanied foreign minors
24 October 2017
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) The LDH, together with other associations and a group of lawyers and jurists, sent an open letter to the President of the French Republic concerning the emergency reception and assessment of presumed unaccompanied minors. The signatories of the letter believe that the creation of a specific and discriminatory support system would be contrary to France’s international commitments. They recall that the law of 14 March 2016 on the protection of children, reaffirms the place of unaccompanied foreign minors within the common law on child protection. As a result, these young people must be considered children, not foreigners. Read more (FR)
