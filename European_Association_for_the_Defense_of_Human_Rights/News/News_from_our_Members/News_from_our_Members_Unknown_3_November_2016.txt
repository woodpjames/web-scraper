The Flemish League for Human rights and its partners grant Facebook with the “Big Brother Award”
3 November 2016

    Français (French) Every year a person, a company, a government agency or a measure is awarded by the “prestigious” Big Brother Award. Who won this shameful price of biggest privacy-offender of the year? A professional jury as well as a broad public elected Facebook as winner. Facebook keeps all of us under surveillance. We do not have any control of what they do with the personal data they collect. And we do not know exactly how much they know about us. Read more (to read in dutch)
