The Association for the Defence of Human Rights in Andalusia (APDHA) – APDHA is mobilizing, in collaboration with various social movements and activists, for the closure of detention centres for foreigners through the campaign “Territory free of Foreigners Detention Centres”
14 November 2017
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) The campaign aims to denounce the existence of these centres and the lamentable conditions of confinement. The organisations denounce the detention of people in these centres while they have not committed any crime. Currently there are seven centres of this kind in Spain and the government has announced the construction of three additional centres. The associations strongly oppose this decision and call on the government to close the existing centres and make far-reaching reforms in migration policies. Read more (ES)
