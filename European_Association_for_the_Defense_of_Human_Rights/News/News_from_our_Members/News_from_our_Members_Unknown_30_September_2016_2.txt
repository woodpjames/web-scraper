    Français (French) Every year a person, an enterprise, a government agency or a measure is awarded the “prestigious” Big Brother Award. This award is named after the character in George Orwell’s novel Nineteen Eighty-Four (1984). Who wins this shameful price of biggest privacy-offender of the year? A professional jury as well as a broad public (you!) decides who receives the award. Read more (only in Flamish)
