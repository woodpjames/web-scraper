The Czech Helsinki Committee supports Ales Bialiatski, Vice-president of FIDH – 6 November 2011
15 November 2011
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) The Czech Helsinki Committee supports Ales Bialiatski, Vice-president of FIDH, who has been detained in Belarus since August 4th, 2011.  The Czech Helsinki Committee calls the civil society organisations and European institutions as well as the Council of Europe to protest against the trial Ales Bialiatski will face and against his detention. Download the appeal of the Czech Helsinki Committee (only available in Czech).
