Belgium: Right to strike, Human right!
23 February 2015
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) 23 February 2015 – In the context of the debates concerning the right to strike and the right to work, the Belgian League of Human Rights reminds that there is no democracy without the right to strike, the latter being essential to its proper functioning. Today, it seems obvious that no hierarchy can be envisioned between human rights. It is their gradual addition over time that forms what we now call democracy. While it has always been threatened and attacked, including in Belgium, violations of the right to strike have reached a new dimension.
