Cyprus: Racist attack and violence against migrant by the police
20 February 2015
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) 20 February 2015 – KISA publicly denounces a new incident of racist violence, inhuman and humiliating treatment against a migrant by members of the police. In view of this new incident, the Cypriot organization reminds that the violence by the police is continuous and deplores the current situation of impunity, as the competent authorities have demonstrated that they are either politically unwilling or unable to bring to justice such illegal actions by members of the police.
