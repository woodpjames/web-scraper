APDHA press release: “Twelve Spanish associations call for a public, universal and free health system for all” – 30 August 2012
19 December 2012
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) The APDHA publishes a press release in which they take a stand on the law 16/2012 that came into force on September 1st and that aims to suppress free access to health care for undocumented migrants in Spain. In a common statement with twelve other associations, the APDHA denounces this reform and supports the Autonomous Communities of Spain and the healthcare professionals that keep treating undocumented migrants, regardless the reform.
