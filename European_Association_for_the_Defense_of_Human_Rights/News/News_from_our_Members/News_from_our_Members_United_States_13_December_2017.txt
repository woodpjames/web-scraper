APDHA, Spain – Expresses its disagreement with Trump’s decision to move the United States Embassy to Jerusalem
13 December 2017
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) Association for the Defence of Human Rights in Andalusia (APDHA) condemns Donald Trump’s unilateral decision, which makes the United States the first country in the world to officially recognise Jerusalem as the capital of the state of Israel. This decision eventually leads to legitimising human rights violations perpetrated against the Palestinian population in the region. Israel has established an institutional system of apartheid – while apartheid is a crime against humanity – has undertaken an illegal colonisation, a militarisation of the territory, resorts to violence and intimidation, and controls resources, including water.
