Etats Généraux des Migrations (FR) – organised its first national session of EGM
27 April 2018
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) 26 – 27 April 2018 – Etats Généraux des Migrations (FR) organised its first national session of EGM which brought together hundreds of people. On the agenda: presentation of citizens’ initiatives responding to unacceptable local situations; intervention of European partners, work on the “common base”, a manifesto bearing values and proposals, resulting from the work of local assemblies.
