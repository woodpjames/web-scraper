Liga voor Mensenrechten – met Fionnuala Ní Aoláin
4 June 2018
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) 4 June 2018 – Liga voor Mensenrechten (BE) met Fionnuala Ní Aoláin, the United Nation special rapporteur on the promotion and protection of human rights and fundamental freedoms while countering terroris. She shares concerns with LIGA in regard to: ethnic profiling, which contributes to anti-terrorist laws and policies that stigmatise particularly the Muslim population; the absence of de-radicalisation programmes in the prison system; ant the challenge for the Belgian authorities to deal with the return of Syrians and their children.
