87th LDH Congress in Niort, 18 May 2013
23 May 2013
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) On May 18, 2013 in Niort, was held the 87th Congress of the French Ligue des Droits de l’Homme (LDH), on the topic of strategic relations between the social economy and human rights. On May 20, an address to the President of the Republic and the parliamentary majority was adopted by the Congress of the LDH in Niort, to remind them of their responsibilities to meet the needs of expanding democracy, and to the satisfaction of social needs.
