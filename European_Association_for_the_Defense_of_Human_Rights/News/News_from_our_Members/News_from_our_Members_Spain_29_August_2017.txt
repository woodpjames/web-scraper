Asociación Pro Derechos Humanos de Andalucía (APDHA), Spain – “Mule ladies”, carrying the injustice on their backs
29 August 2017
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) Two women carrying heavy loads of goods from Ceuta to Morocco were trampled to death by the crowd gathered in front of the border post. Our member organisation in Andalusia, APDHA, reiterates its appeal to the Spanish, Moroccan and European governments to implement measures that will guarantee respect for the dignity and physical integrity of these women.
