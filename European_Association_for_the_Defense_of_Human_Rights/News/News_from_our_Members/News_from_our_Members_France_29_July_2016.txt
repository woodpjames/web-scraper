LDH France criticises the extension of the State of Emergency
29 July 2016
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) 21 July 2016 – The French Human Rights League (LDH) denounces the measures taken to respond to the acts of violence of these last months, in particular the decision of the Parliament to extend the State of emergency for the fourth time. LDH considers that these measures are useless and dangerous because they have proved to be ineffective to fight against terrorism and they violate fundamental freedoms and democracy.
