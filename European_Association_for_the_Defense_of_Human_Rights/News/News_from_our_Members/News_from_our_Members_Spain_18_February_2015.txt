Spain: Accusation of policemen involved in El Tarajal case: towards the restoration of the dignity of the victims and their families
18 February 2015
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) 12 February 2015 – After one year of stalemate in the investigation on the death of 15 migrants one year ago on the border between Morocco and the Spanish enclave of Ceuta, 16  Guardia Civil police officers involved in the case have been accused by judges from Ceuta. Until now, the procedure was characterised by a lack of policitical will regarding the establishment of the facts and the determination of liability. Read APDHA press release (in Spanish only)
