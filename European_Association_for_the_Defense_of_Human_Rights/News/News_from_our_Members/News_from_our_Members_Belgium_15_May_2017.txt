    Français (French) Are we giving up our hard-won freedoms for the illusion of security? More and more measures taken in the name of safety limit our freedom. How much freedom do we want to give up for safety? This is an issue on which our Belgian member, the Flemish-speaking League for Human Rights, will debate with politicians, journalists, experts and the public. Read more (only available in Flemish)
