French Human Rights League co-initiates a petition “stop to multinational companies’ impunity”
16 September 2016
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) 12 September 2016 – The French Human Rights League (LDH) in collaboration with other French associations has launched a petition encouraging France and the European Union to support the establishment of a UN treaty on the respect of human rights by multinational companies. In a globalised world where multinational companies have great influence and power, LDH highlights the lack of international binding mechanisms able to frame their activities whereas they affect the life of millions of people, the future of our planet and are responsible of human rights abuses.
