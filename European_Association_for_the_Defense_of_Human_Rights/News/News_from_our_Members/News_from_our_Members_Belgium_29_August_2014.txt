Belgium: The Liga voor Mensenrechten organizes a Student Award – August 2014
29 August 2014
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) The Liga voor Mensenrechten, our belgian  member, organizes a Student Award in order to reward a student who wrote  an outstanding academic paper about Human Rights. The winner will have a  publication in the Liga’s journal and will receive 500€. The deadline  is the 31st of August 2014. For more information, please visit the  Liga’s website.
