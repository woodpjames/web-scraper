LIDU offers its support to the wife of the Nigerian refugee murdered during a racist aggression
29 July 2016
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) 7 July 2016 – Following the tragic events in Fermo where the Nigerian refugee Emmanuel Chidi Namdi was beaten to death during a racist aggression, the Italian League for Human Rights (LIDU) sent a letter of condolence to his wife offering her its support and legal assistance. Furthermore, LIDU was concerned by the fact that many of these heinous crimes and racist acts remain unpunished. Read more, only in Italian
