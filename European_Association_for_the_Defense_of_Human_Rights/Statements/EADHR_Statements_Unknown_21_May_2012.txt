The EU-US PNR Agreement adopted by the majority of MEPs is a threat to citizens’ rights – Press release 21 May 2012
21 May 2012
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) In an unexpected move, on April 19th 2012 the European Parliament adopted the EU-US PNR Agreement concerning the transmission of flight passenger data to the American authorities. AEDH deeply regrets these developments, which go against previous parliamentary votes. This will have serious consequences on EU citizens’ rights, which AEDH has repeatedly denounced.
