Contribution to the consultation of the European Commission on EU citizenship
6 April 2016
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) 8 December 2015 – AEDH has contributed to the consultation of the European Commission on EU citizenship. In its contribution, AEDH has underlined the urgent need to respect and protect the rights of EU citizens, in particular the freedom of movement, through the use of appropriate mechanisms. The document is only available in French.
