Joint open letter to the Commissioner for Migration and Home Affairs on migrant children’s rights
8 December 2014
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) In an open letter, a group of 26 organisations, including AEDH, working to improve the protection of children’s rights asks the new Commissioner for Migration and Home Affairs, Commissioner Avramopoulos, to personally commit to take measures to realise the rights of every child affected by migration, regardless of their or their parents’ migration status, and to address all violations of their rights, as a priority within his mandate. Read the joint open letter to Commissioner Avramopoulos. Read the joint letter addressing related recommendations to the European Council, dated 22 May 2014.
