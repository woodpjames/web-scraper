Lampedusa tragic shipwreck: a wake-up call for the European Union?
9 October 2013
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French)  AEDH, FIDH (International Federation for Human Rights), EMHRN (Euro-Mediterranean Human Rights Network) and Migreurop have addressed an open letter to the EU Interior Ministers and to the Commissioner for Home Affaires Cecilia Malmström. On the occasion of the Council of the EU, we require a radical change in the European approach to migration, which places preventing loss of life and respect for human rights at its core.
