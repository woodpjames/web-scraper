Joint open letter to the European Council calling for European commitments on children’s rights to be prioritised in future common migration and asylum policies
22 May 2014
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) Joint open letter to the European Council calling for European commitments on children’s rights to be prioritised in future common migration and asylum policies Brussels, 22 May In the view of the upcoming June European Council, where strategic guidelines will be adopted outlining future EU Home Affairs policies, we, the undersigned organisations, children’s ombudspersons of Europe and the United Nations Committee on the Rights of the Child, are writing to urge member states to build on previous commitments to children and make child rights a priority.  For more information, please download the Open Letter.
