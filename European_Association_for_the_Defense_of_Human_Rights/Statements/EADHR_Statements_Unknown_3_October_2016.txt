Arrest of the president of the Belgian human rights league : AEDH, FIDH join LDH to lodge a complaint against X
3 October 2016
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) Following the arrest of the Belgian League of Human Rights President Alexis Deswaef on the 2nd of April, FIDH, AEDH, and Belgian LDH have lodged a complaint against X. This administrative arrest issued by the Brussels police on the square of the Bourse in Brussels, during an antifascist and antiracist  gathering, took place in the context of the commemorations following the Brussels attacks.
