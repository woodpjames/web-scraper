The asylum and the rise of racist violence in Greece – Press release, June 3rd 2009
3 June 2009
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) The Hellenic League for Human Rights (HLHR) and the European League for Human Rights (AEDH) express their deep concern about the emergency of the asylum system and the rise of xenophobia and racist violence in Greece. HLHR and AEDH propose policy solutions and immediate remedy action in order to avoid escalation of phenomena of violation of human rights with a highly negative impact on victims and society. The press release is available in English, Greek, Spanish and French.
