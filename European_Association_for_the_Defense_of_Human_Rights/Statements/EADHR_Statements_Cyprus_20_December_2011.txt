Second Trial Observation Mission in Cyprus: As Doros Polycarpou’s prosecution continues, international attention and support intensify – Inter-associations press release, 13 December 2011
20 December 2011
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) On the occasion of the first hearing of the trial against Doros Polykarpou, Executive Director of KISA(Action for Equality, Support and Anti-racism), scheduled on 12 December 2011, in Larnaca, Cyprus, the Euro-Mediterranean Human Rights Network (EMHRN), The Platform for International Cooperation on Undocumented Migrants (PICUM), The European Network Against Racism (ENAR), Migreurop and The European Association for Human Rights (AEDH) have sent a second trial observation mission. Several meetings had been arranged with the competent Cypriot authorities to express our on-going concern regarding the criminalisation of Mr Polycarpou, accused of rioting during the Rainbow Festival 2010 in Larnaca, accusations which the delegation believes are unfounded.
