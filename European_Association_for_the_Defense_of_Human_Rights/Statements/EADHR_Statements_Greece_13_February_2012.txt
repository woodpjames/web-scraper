Greece: a suicide of “increasing austerity” – Press release 13 February 2012
13 February 2012
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) The European Association for the Defence of Human Rights (AEDH), the Hellenic League for Human Rights (HLHR) and the French League of Human Rights, (LDH), consider that austerity measures imposed on Greece lead the society to a dramatic and suicidal situation. More reforms, more economy, more austerity. Mainly more austerity. The message sent to Greece by the European Union, the European Central Bank and the IMF can be summarized up as follows: In order for the Greek government to qualify for the financial aid of 130 billion, it is ordered, roughly, to put in order its house on three levels.
