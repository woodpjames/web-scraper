AEDH comments on the Data Protection Day – Press release 28th January 2013
29 January 2013
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) The Data Protection Day is an opportunity for AEDH to underline the need not to derogate from the rule of the protection of private life and individual liberties constituted by the use of personal data for a specific purpose and for a limited storage period. The abuse of personal data is regrettably too often found either for commercial use or for law enforcement or justice purposes. Download the press release here in English and in French.
