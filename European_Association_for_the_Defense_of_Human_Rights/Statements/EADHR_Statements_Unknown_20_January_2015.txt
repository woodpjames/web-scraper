Aurore Martin: Open letter to the French Minister of justice co-signed by LDH, AEDH, EMHRN and FIDH
20 January 2015
In a open letter to Madam Chritiane Taubira – the French Minister of justice – the LDH, the AEDH, the REMDH and the FIDH condemn Aurore Martin’s arrest. This young French woman is currently detained in Spain under a European arrest warrant issued by the Spanish courts for offences not punishable in France and for which the French courts haven’t authorised her surrender to the Spanish authorities.
