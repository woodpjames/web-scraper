Gender equality:    is progress finally underway for the European Union? – Press release 8 March 2012
8 March 2012
This post is also available in: 
    Français (French) The European Association for the Defence of Human Rights deplores the persistenceof gender inequality within Europe. The economic and financial crisis has served to aggravate the situation for women, who are already most at risk of unemployment, poverty and job insecurity. Still largely excluded in terms of political representation and professional responsibilities, in different countries women are seeing the right to control their own fertility dangerously challenged.
