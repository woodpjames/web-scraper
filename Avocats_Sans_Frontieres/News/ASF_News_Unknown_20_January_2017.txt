Thank you for your encouragement!
20 January 2017
20 January 2017Brussels, 20 January 2017 – At the end of December 2016, we challenged you with this question: “What about you? What will you do to promote human rights in 2017?” Many of you responded, for example by making a donation to Avocats Sans Frontières. But also by sending us your encouragement and your messages. We would like to share some of these with you.Whichever way you have chosen to get involved, we thank you.We wish you the best for 2017.The ASF Team***In 2017, ASF will be celebrating its 25 years of commitment to human rights.What about you? What will you do for human rights?
