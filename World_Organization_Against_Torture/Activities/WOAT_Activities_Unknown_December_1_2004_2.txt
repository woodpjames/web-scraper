Committee on the Elimination of Discrimination Against Women: 32nd session from January 10th to 28th 2005 in New York
December  1, 2004
Save as PDF Print versionViolence against women / Events / 2004 / DecemberCommittee on the Elimination of Discrimination Against Women: 32nd session from January 10th to 28th 2005 in New YorkIndependent experts of the Committee on the Elimination of Discrimination against Women will meet from January 10th to 28th 2005 at United Nations Headquarters in New York to consider the periodic reports of Algeria, Croatia, Gabon, Italy, Lao People’s Democratic Republic, Paraguay, Samoa and Turkey.
