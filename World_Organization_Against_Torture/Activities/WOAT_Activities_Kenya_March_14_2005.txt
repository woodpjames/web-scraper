Kenya, 14th March 2005, briefing session with the HRC's members in New York
March 14, 2005
Save as PDF Print versionMonitoring protection mechanisms / Events / Kenya / 2005 / MarchKenya: Kenya, 14th March 2005, briefing session with the HRC's members in New YorkThe NGOs based in Kenya "The Kenya Section of the International Commission of Jurists (ICJ Kenya)", "The Coalition on Violence Against Women – Kenya (COVAW-K)", "Kenya Alliance for Advancement of Children (KAACR)" and "The Child Rights Advisory Documentation and Legal Centre (The CRADLE)" will submit, in collaboration with OMCT, an alternative report on State violence in Kenya to the Human Rights Committee's members. The Committee will consider Kenya's official report on March 14th and 15th in New York.
