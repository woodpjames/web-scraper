Pakistan:  Alternative Report to the Human Rights Committee
July  7, 2017
Save as PDF Print versionMonitoring protection mechanisms / Reports and Publications / Pakistan / 2017 / JulyPakistan: Alternative Report to the Human Rights Committee​​The following submission has been prepared by the Justice Project Pakistan(JPP), World Organisation Against Torture (OMCT), and Reprieve for submission to the 120th session of the Human Rights Committee (HRC). The report comments on the Government of Pakistan’s Initial Report under the International Covenant on Civil and Political Rights (ICCPR) particularly with regards to its failure to fulfill its obligations with respect to its application of the death penalty and police torture. The report supplements the information contained in the submission made by the parties to the HRC for the List of Issues (LOIs) and is meant to be read in conjunction thereof.You may read the report here: http://tbinternet.ohchr.org/Treaties/CCPR/Shared%20Documents/PAK/INT_CCPR_CSS_PAK_27601_E.pdf or in the file attached.
