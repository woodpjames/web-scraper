Save as PDF Print versionRights of the child / Reports and Publications / Israel / 2016 / MayIsrael: Alternative Report on Torture and Ill-Treatment of Palestinian ChildrenThe OMCT in collaboration with the Defence for Children International-Palestine submitted a joint alternative report to the UN Committee against Torture to inform its review of the fifth periodic report of Israel at the 57th Session. The Committee against Torture will review Israel on Tuesday, 3 May 2016 (10:00- 13:00) and Wednesday, 4 May 2016 (15:00-18:00). The session will be webcasted and can be followed live under www.treatybodywebcast.org.For the joint report please see attached file.
