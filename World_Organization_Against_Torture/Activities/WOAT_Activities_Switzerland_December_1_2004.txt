Save as PDF Print versionMonitoring protection mechanisms / Events / Switzerland / 2004 / DecemberSwitzerland: Committee on the Elimination of Racial Discrimination : 66th session from February 21st to March 11th 2005 in GenevaIndependent experts of the Committee on the Elimination of Racial Discrimination will meet from February 21st to March 11th 2005 in Geneva to consider the periodic reports of Australia, Azerbaijan, Bahrain, Bosnia-Herzegovina, Ethiopia, France, Ireland, Luxembourg, Nicaragua, Nigeria, Papua New Guinea, Lao People’s Democratic Republic and El Salvador.
