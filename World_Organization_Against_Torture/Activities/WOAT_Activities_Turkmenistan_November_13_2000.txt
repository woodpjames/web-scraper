Turkmenistan:  Turkmenistan: arrest of Nikolai Nikolaevich Gherasimov
November 13, 2000
Save as PDF Print versionUrgent campaigns / Urgent Interventions / Turkmenistan / 2000 / NovemberTurkmenistan: Turkmenistan: arrest of Nikolai Nikolaevich GherasimovCase TKM 131100  The International Secretariat of OMCT requests your URGENT intervention in the following situation in Turkmenistan.  Brief description of the situation  The International Secretariat of OMCT has been informed by the Kyrgyz Committee for Human Rights (KCHR), a member of the network, that Mr Nikolai Nikolaevich Gherasimov, a journalist working for the Azerbaijani Information Agency, was reportedly arrested between 6 and 7 November 2000 and is currently detained in prison in Krasnovodsk town.  Gherasimov is a citizen of Turkmenistan and a former correspondent of the “Neitralni Turkmenistan” (Neutral Turkmenistan) newspaper.  The International Secretariat is gravely concerned for his physical and psychological integrity, particularly as detention facilities in Turkmenistan have been described as “terrible”.  Furthermore, the KCHR, reported that a number of deaths in detention in such facilities, have been the result of inhuman conditions of detention.  Action requested  Please write to the authorities in Turkmenistan urging them to:  i.	take all necessary measures to guarantee the physical and psychological integrity of Mr Nikolai Nikolaevich Gherasimov and order his immediate release in the absence of valid legal charges or, if such charges exist, bring him before an impartial and competent tribunal and guarantee their procedural rights at all times; ii.	guarantee the respect of human rights and the fundamental freedoms throughout the country in accordance with national laws and international human rights standards.
