Save as PDF Print versionReports and Publications / 2017 / AugustOMCT E-bulletin: 61th session of the Committee Against Torture - July/August 2017E-Bulletin English8 pages / 319 KBThe  e-bulletin on the 61st session of the Committee against Torture, which took place from 24 July to 11 August 2017, is out. During this session, the Committee considered the State reports submitted by Paraguay, Ireland and Panama. It also reviewed Antigua and Barbuda in the absence of a state report. The e-bulletin is available in English, French, Spanish and Arabic.
