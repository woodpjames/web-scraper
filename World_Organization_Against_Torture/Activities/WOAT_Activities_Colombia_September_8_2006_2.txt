Colombia: Assassination de M.Edgar Emiro Fajardo Marulanda
September  8, 2006
Save as PDF Print versionUrgent campaigns / Urgent Interventions / Colombia / 2006 / SeptemberColombia: Colombia: Assassination de M.Edgar Emiro Fajardo MarulandaCase COL 080906 Assassination Geneva, September 8, 2006  The International Secretariat of the World Organisation Against Torture (OMCT) requests your URGENT intervention in the following situation in Colombia. Description of the situation:The International Secretariat of OMCT has received with deep concern information from the United Workers’ Federation (Central Unitaria de Trabajadores - CUT), a member of OMCT network, about the assassination of Mr. Edgar Emiro Fajardo Marulanda, Professor in Statistics and Development of Critic Thinking of the 20th Century at the college “Universidad Cooperativa de Colombia”, in Bogotá.  According to the information received, on September 1, 2006, around 3:30 a.m., Mr. Edgar Emiro Fajardo Marulanda was assaulted by unknown persons who seriously wounded him with three official armoured projectiles, in front of his apartment, in San Ignacio residential estate, San Mateo district, annex city of Soacha. Brought to the hospital, he died a few hours later.  Economist and Master in Philosophy,  Mr. Fajardo Marulanda used to work at the Engineering Faculty in the “Universidad Cooperativa de Colombia” and to teach in many colleges in Bogotá.  The International Secretariat of OMCT strongly condemns this assassination, as well as the persecution against the civil population and the obvious impunity of perpetrators.   Action requested: Please write to the authorities in Colombia urging them to: i.	order a thorough and impartial investigation into the circumstances of the assassination of Mr. Edgar Emiro Fajardo Marulanda, in order to identify those responsible, to bring them to justice and to apply the civil, penal and/or administrative sanctions provided for by law; ii.	take all necessary measures to guarantee the physical and psychological integrity of all members of the family of Mr. Edgar Emiro Fajardo Marulanda; iii.	guarantee that adequate reparation is provided to Mr. Edgar Emiro Fajardo Marulanda’s family; iv.	immediately comply with the recommendations of international and regional human rights agencies, including the Office of the United Nations High Commissioner for Human Rights; v.	more generally, ensure the respect of human rights and fundamental freedoms throughout the country in accordance with national laws and international human rights standards.
