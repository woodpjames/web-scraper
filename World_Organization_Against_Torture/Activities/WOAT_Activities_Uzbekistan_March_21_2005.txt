March 21, 2005
Save as PDF Print versionMonitoring protection mechanisms / Events / Uzbekistan / 2005 / MarchUzbekistan: Uzbekistan, 21st March 2005, briefing session with the HRC's members in New YorkThe NGOs based in Uzbekistan "Legal Aid Society (LAS)", "Women’s Resource Centre (WRC)" and "The Initiative-taking Youth Group (Orzu)" will submit, in collaboration with OMCT, an alternative report on State violence in Uzbekistan to the Human Rights Committee's members. The Committee will consider Uzbekistan's official report on March 21st and 22nd in New York.
