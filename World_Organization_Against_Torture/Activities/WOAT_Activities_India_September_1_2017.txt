India:  MASUM monthly human rights report - August 2017
September  1, 2017
Save as PDF Print versionReports and Publications / India / 2017 / SeptemberIndia: MASUM monthly human rights report - August 2017Banglar Manabadhikar Surakksha Mancha (MASUM) conducted 11 regular fact findings during
August 2017 and made complaints before the relevant authorities and human rights institutions
and 3 complaints were made before the National Human Rights Commission. Out of 11 regular fact findings and subsequent complaints, four were about extra
judicial killings, one about torture committed by Border Security Force personnel, three about
illegal detentions of Bangladeshi women and children at Indian prison and three about police
torture.
