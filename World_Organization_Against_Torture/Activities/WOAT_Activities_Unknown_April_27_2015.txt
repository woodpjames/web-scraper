Macedonia:  Joint Shadow Report to the Committee against Torture

Save as PDF Print versionMonitoring protection mechanisms / Reports and Publications / Macedonia / 2015 / AprilMacedonia: Joint Shadow Report to the Committee against Torture CAT Shadow Report Macedonia16 pages / 628 KBThe Helsinki Committee for Human Rights of the Republic of Macedonia (MHC) in collaboration with the World Organisation Against Torture (OMCT), the Institute for Human Rights (IHR),  the Healthy Options Project Skopje (HOPS), REACТOR – Research in Action, the Association for Health Education and Research (HERA), and the Coalition 'Sexual and Health Rights of Marginalized Communities' (CSHRMC) submitted a joint shadow report to
the UN Committee against Torture to inform its review of the third periodic report of the former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia at the 54th Session. The Committee against Torture will review the former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia on Monday 4 May 2015 (10:00 - 12:00) and on Tuesday 5 May 2015 (15:00 - 18:00). The session will be webcasted and can be followed live under www.treatybodywebcast.org.
