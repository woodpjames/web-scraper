During the session the Committee will examine the reports of 15 States parties: Austria, Azerbaijan, Colombia, Greece, India, Kazakhstan, Maldives, Namibia, Netherlands, Nicaragua, Peru, Poland, Suriname, Vietnam, and Tajikistan.  For more information, visit http://www.un.org/womenwatch/daw/cedaw/37sess.htm.
