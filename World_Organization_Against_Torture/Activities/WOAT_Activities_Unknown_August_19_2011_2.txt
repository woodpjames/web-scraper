Defend the defenders: Screening of OMCT film Indifference and Impunity in Zurich
August 19, 2011
Save as PDF Print versionHuman rights defenders / Events / 2011 / AugustDefend the defenders: Screening of OMCT film Indifference and Impunity in Zurich  
On August 11, 2011, OMCT film "Indifference and Impunity" was screened in Zurich at the occasion of a public encounter on violence against women organized by the Swiss Agency for Development and Cooperation.High Commissionner for Human Rights Navi Pillay and President of the Swiss Confederation Micheline Calmy-Rey participated to the event. Defender Justine Masika Bihamba and her sponsor Swiss clown Dimitri were present to introduce the film.Indifference and Impunity (English version)Ignoriert und Vogelfrei (deutsche Version)
