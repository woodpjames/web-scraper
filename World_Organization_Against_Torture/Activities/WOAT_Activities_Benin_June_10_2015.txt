Benin:  OMCT calls the CRC to protect children from torture
June 10, 2015
Save as PDF Print versionMonitoring protection mechanisms / Reports and Publications / Benin / 2015 / JuneBenin: OMCT calls the CRC to protect children from torture​The OMCT, represented by its local partner in Benin ESAM, has presented an alternative report to the Committee on the Rights of the Child and called the Committee to pay particular attention to the situation of children deprived of liberty and their protection from torture and ill-treatment.Please find attached the Alternative Report on all articles of the CRC and the special document on Juvenile Justice.
