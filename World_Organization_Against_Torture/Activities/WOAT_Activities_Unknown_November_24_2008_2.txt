Save as PDF Print versionViolence against women / Events / 2008 / November16 DAYS OF ACTIVISM AGAINST GENDER VIOLENCE - NOVEMBER 25 - DECEMBER 10, 2008Human Rights for Women – Human Rights for All: Celebrating 60 years of the Universal Declaration of Human Rights The 2008 “16 Days of Activism Against Gender Violence” Campaign dedicates this year’s theme to the 60th anniversary of the Universal Declaration of Human Rights and the recognition that human rights cannot be universal without human rights for women.  2008 also marks the 15th anniversary of the Declaration on the Elimination of Violence against Women and the Vienna Declaration (see Background). You will find below OMCT has posted on its website selected information on activities members of the SOS-Torture Network and other partners will be carrying out during the 2008 16 Days Campaign.
