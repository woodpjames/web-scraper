Capitol Hill Briefing on Responding to the Syrian Humanitarian and Refugee Crises
 Thursday, February 27, 2014 
Please join us on Tuesday, March 4 in Washington, DC for a briefing on responding to the Syrian humanitarian and refugee crises. A panel of experts from CVT, the National Consortium of Torture Treatment Programs, the International Rescue Committee, and the Refugee Council USA will provide information on the current status of the crises and offer recommendations for actions the United States can take to alleviate the suffering of Syrians living inside the country and those living as refugees in the region. Additional details are available here.If you do attend, we look forward to your comments.
