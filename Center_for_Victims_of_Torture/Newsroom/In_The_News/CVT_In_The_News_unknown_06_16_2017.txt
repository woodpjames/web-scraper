ANNOUNCING: 2017 Drucker Prize Semifinalists
06/16/2017
Podcast: Play in new window | Download Subscribe: Apple Podcasts | Android | RSS Laura Pizer Gueron works at the Centre for Victims of Torture in Minnesota, U.S. We delve into her experience working with survivors of torture and trauma and she sheds some light on areas to consider when working with this population. Not only does she identify important signs and symptoms, but she discusses important resources for anyone working with this population. You won’t want to miss this insightful and engaging interview!
