When medical people abet torture
07/09/2013
A doctor and professor at the University of Minnesota has created a new website called  "Doctors Who Torture." Its founder, Dr. Steven Miles, says the site 
"characterizes the practices of countries as having systematic punishment of physicians who torture, punishment of a few token torture physicians or having a record of allowing physicians who abet torture complete impunity from punishment."
