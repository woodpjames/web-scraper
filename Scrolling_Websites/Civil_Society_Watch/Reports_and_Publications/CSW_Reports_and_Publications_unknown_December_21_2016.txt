BRIEF REPORT: WIDENING SPACE BY YOUNG HUMAN RIGHTS DEFENDERS
December 21, 2016
On 21 November 2016, CIVICUS together with Amnesty  International, Defence  for  Children  International,  KidsRights and World Vision convened a side event- Widening Space by Young Human Rights Defenders. The event allowed young human rights defenders to share their local realities and the ways in which they contribute to the protection and promotion of human rights, in their communities and globally. This report summarises key insights, discussions, and outcomes from the event.Read the report in English.
