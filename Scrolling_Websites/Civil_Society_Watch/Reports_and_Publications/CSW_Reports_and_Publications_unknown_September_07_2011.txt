Cutting the Diamonds: Civil Society Index 2008-2011
September 07, 2011
This report provides the first description of the quantitative data from the second implementation phase (2008 to 2011) of CIVICUS' Civil Society Index, bringing together the information from a set of 25 countries for which the data was finalised at the time of writing.
