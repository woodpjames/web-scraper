Human Rights, Participation and the 2030 Agenda
February 14, 2019
By Mandeep Tiwana, CIVICUS Chief Programmes Officer This January, the UN organised a much-needed dialogue in Geneva on the link between human rights and Agenda 2030 for Sustainable Development. The discussion’s timeliness is brought home by the actions of right wing populists and authoritarian leaders who together with regressive anti-rights movements are seeking to roll back the clock on human rights progress.
