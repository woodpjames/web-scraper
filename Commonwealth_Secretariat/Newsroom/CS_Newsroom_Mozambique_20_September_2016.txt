Mozambique's hand of friendship to the Commonwealth
20 September 2016
The president of Mozambique has invited the Secretary-General to visit his country. The invitation was made during a meeting of the two leaders at the UN General Assembly today. Among the issues they discussed were trade, empowering women and children and the contribution they can make to driving the economy.
