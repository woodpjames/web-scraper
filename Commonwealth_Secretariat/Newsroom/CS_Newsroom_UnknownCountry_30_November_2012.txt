Youth and women: HIV vulnerability
30 November 2012
Dr Watts presented at a World AIDS Day Commemoration at the Commonwealth Secretariat on 30 November 2012. The STRIVE research consortium involves six partner organisations, including the London School of Hygiene and Tropical Medicine, which investigate the social norms and inequalities that drive HIV. Dr Watts also heads the Social and Mathematical Epidemiology Group in the Department of Global Health and Development at the London School of Hygiene and Tropical Medicine.
