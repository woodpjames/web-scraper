The Fifth Commonwealth Public Sector Ministers’ Forum
UnknownDate
The Commonwealth will be convening its fifth Public Administration Ministers’ Forum in parallel with the Commonwealth Association for Public Administration and Management (CAPAM) 5th Biennial Conference in Putrajaya, Malaysia Ministers, Heads of Delegation and Senior Government Officials from Ministries of Public and Governance or other relevant Ministries will be in attendance. REGISTRATION Each member country wishing to attend the Commonwealth Public Sector Ministers Forum should submit one registration form for its delegation. Access to the meeting room will be limited to the Minister/Head of Delegation plus one accompanying delegate. The information provided will serve as the basis for compiling the Directory of Delegations. Please list all delegates with full title and in protocol order.
