The 12th African Heads of Public Service Forum will be held in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania from 13-15 July 2015. The theme for the Forum is ‘From Good Plans to Effective Execution: Anchoring Policy, Strategy and Execution for a High Performance Culture in the Public Sector’. The Forum of Commonwealth Heads of African Public Services is the largest annual gathering of Heads of Public Services from Commonwealth Africa. It represents an unparalleled opportunity for delegates to share their knowledge and discover the latest challenges and opportunities to managing an effective, efficient and accountable Public Service.
