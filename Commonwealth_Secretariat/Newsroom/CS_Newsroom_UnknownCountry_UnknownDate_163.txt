The 20th Commonwealth Games will be held in Glasgow, Scotland, where 6,500 athletes from 71 countries and territories will compete in 17 sports over 11 days. The Commonwealth Games is a unique, world class event which is held once every four years. It is often referred to as the ‘Friendly Games’. The Commonwealth Games Federation (CGF) is the organisation that is responsible for the direction and control of the Commonwealth Games. For more information on Glasgow 2014, visit www.glasgow2014.com
