Cameroon Presidential Election 2011: Final Report
22 December 2011
At the invitation of the Minister of External Relations of Cameroon, the Commonwealth Secretary-General, Mr Kamalesh Sharma, constituted an Expert Team for the 2011 Cameroon Presidential Election. The Team was led by Mr Frederick Mitchell MP, Former Foreign Minister of The Bahamas, and comprised six experts in total. The Expert Team was supported by a four member staff team from the Commonwealth Secretariat.
