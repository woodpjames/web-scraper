Travel bursaries for broadcast skills exchange
2 January 2014
The Commonwealth Broadcasting Association (CBA) are providing funding for eight employees of CBA member organisations to visit another country in 2014. The bursary is provided for a media project that would benefit the bursar’s organisation and society, which must include a placement at a CBA member broadcaster in the country being visited. Last year, broadcasters from India, Nigeria, Zambia and Jamaica went on exchange to Canada, Rwanda, Kenya and South Africa respectively. The application deadline is 14 March 2014. Details
