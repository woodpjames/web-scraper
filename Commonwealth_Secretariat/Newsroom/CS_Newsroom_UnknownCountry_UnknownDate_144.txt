17th Commonwealth Lecture, 9 April 2015

The Rt Hon Helen Clark, Administrator, United Nations Development Programme and former Prime Minister of New Zealand will be speaking on 'Youth, Innovation and Sustainable Development: the Commonwealth in a Post-2015 World'. The lecture will be followed by a reception, hosted by the City of London in the Old Library, also at the Guildhall. You will need your ticket and photographic identification to access the Guildhall.
