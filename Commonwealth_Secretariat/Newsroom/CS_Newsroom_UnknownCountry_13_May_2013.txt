Commonwealth Games Glasgow 2014 announces ticketing process
13 May 2013
Around one million tickets will be up for grabs. The 20th Commonwealth Games in Glasgow, Scotland, will see teams from around the Commonwealth compete in 17 sports from 23 July to 3 August 2014. The Commonwealth Games Federation is responsible for the direction and control of the Commonwealth Games. For more on the ticketing process visit the Glasgow 2014 website.
