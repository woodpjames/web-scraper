Trade and aid go hand in hand, says Secretary-General
4 November 2016
Boosting international trade, combating climate change and tackling violence against women are among the Commonwealth Secretary-General’s top priorities. In a feature interview published today for Devex.com, a news website for the international development community, Secretary-General Patricia Scotland tells journalist Gabriella Jóźwiak about her ambitious agenda for action. Trade should support global efforts to lift countries out of poverty, she explains, outlining her vision for a Commonwealth which supports good governance, human rights and the empowerment of young people.
