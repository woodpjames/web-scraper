Commonwealth Youth Council seeks nominations for Executive
2 October 2013
The Commonwealth Youth Council is seeking nominations for its Executive positions including Chairperson, Vice Chairpersons and Regional Representatives. Nominees must be a member of a national youth council or a registered youth-led organisation and aged 15-27. Nominations are open until 7 October 2013 for the positions: The Commonwealth Youth Council is an autonomous youth-led body, which aims to further advance the youth development agenda and strengthen young people’s participation in the Commonwealth. Its Executive will be responsible for the leadership and strategic direction of the Council. The first General Assembly of the Council will be held during the Commonwealth Youth Forum in Sri Lanka from 10 to 14 November 2013.
