The Commonwealth Ministerial Action Group (CMAG) to meet  in London
 6 March 2015
The Commonwealth Ministerial Action Group (CMAG) comprising foreign ministers from nine member countries will meet at the Commonwealth Secretariat in London, UK, on 12 March 2015. CMAG was mandated by Commonwealth Heads of Government in 2011 to act as the custodian of Commonwealth political values.
