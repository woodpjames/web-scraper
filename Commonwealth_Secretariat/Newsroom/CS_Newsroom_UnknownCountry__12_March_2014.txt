Commonwealth Ministerial Action Group, meeting, London
 12 March 2014
The Group, which consists of nine foreign ministers, deals with serious or persistent violations of the Commonwealth’s fundamental political values. Friday's meeting will be followed by a media conference at Marlborough House. Details are provided in the note to editors below. The media conference is scheduled to take place at 12:30pm, immediately following the conclusion of the Commonwealth Ministerial Action Group meeting. Location: The Commonwealth Secretariat
Marlborough House, Pall Mall
