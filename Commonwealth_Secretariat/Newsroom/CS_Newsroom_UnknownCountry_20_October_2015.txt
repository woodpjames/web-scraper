Education Hub launches discussion on achieving quality education
20 October 2015
The Commonwealth Education Hub is a virtual one-stop knowledge service for Commonwealth education professionals and policy-makers. Launched in June 2015 the Hub has hosted a number of community discussions. The latest explores action towards achieving Sustainable Development Goal 4: Quality Education - Ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all. Mr Vis Naidoo, a board member of Education without Borders, and past Vice President of the Commonwealth of Learning will guest moderate the discussion. To participate in this virtual discussion and to debate or identify insights and issues relating to SDG 4, please join the Education Hub and Community of Practice New discussion on #Commonwealth #EducationHub: Exploring #SDG 4.
