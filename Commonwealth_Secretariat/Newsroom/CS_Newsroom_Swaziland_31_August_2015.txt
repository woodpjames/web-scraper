Swaziland bus crash a tragedy - Secretary-General
31 August 2015
Commonwealth Secretary-General Kamalesh Sharma today expressed his deep condolences to the families and loved ones of the young women who lost their lives in a bus accident in Swaziland on Friday. Speaking from Fiji where he is on an official visit, Mr Sharma described the incident as a tragedy. The Secretary-General said: “I was deeply saddened to learn of the horrific accident. My heart goes out to the families and friends of those who died, and to those who are injured.”
