St Vincent and The Grenadines Foreign Minister praises Small States Conference
4 April 2014
The Foreign Minister of St Vincent and The Grenadines Camillo Gonzalves on 3 April 2014 spoke on Jamaica’s Hot 102 FM radio station about the recently concluded Third Biennial Global Conference on Small States, convened by The Commonwealth. During the interview, Mr Gonzalves highlights the value of the meeting and the role played by The Commonwealth: "They Commonwealth Secretariat has done a lot of work in thinking about the challenges of small states and it has a number of innovative ideas. [The Secretariat] was very useful in convening the right mix of people and in helping steer the conversation towards practical outcomes." Mr Gonzalves also spoke about ways to address the development concerns of small states.
