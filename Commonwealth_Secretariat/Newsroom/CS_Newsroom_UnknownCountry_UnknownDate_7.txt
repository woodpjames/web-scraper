UnknownDate
The meeting of Law Ministers and Attorneys-General of Small Commonwealth Jurisdictions (LMSCJ) will take place from 12-13 September 2013 at Marlborough House, London. This meeting takes place immediately following the Senior Officials of Law Ministries (SOLM) Meeting, which occurs every three years in London. The LMSCJ meeting's theme is Enhancing democratic governance and the rule of law in small Commonwealth jurisdictions.
