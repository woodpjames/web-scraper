Commonwealth Secretary-General commiserates with Government and people of Bangladesh
20 March 2013
Mr Sharma said: “It is with profound sadness that I learnt this morning of the death of President Rahman. He was a beloved and respected leader of Bangladesh and his death is an irreparable loss to the country. “On behalf of the Commonwealth family and on my own behalf, I would like to convey our deepest condolences to the family of the late President and to the Government and people of Bangladesh.
