Report of the Commonwealth Expert Team - Namibia Presidential and National Assembly Elections
28 November 2014
In response to an invitation from the Electoral Commission of Namibia (ECN), the Commonwealth Secretary-General constituted an Expert Team for the Presidential and Parliamentary Elections of 28 November 2014. The Commonwealth Expert Team was led by Ms Artemisa Franco, Executive Director, Centre for Human Rights and Development, and former Commissioner, National Electoral Commission of Mozambique.
