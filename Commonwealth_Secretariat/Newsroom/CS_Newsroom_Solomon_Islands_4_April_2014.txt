Commonwealth Secretary-General conveys sympathy to Solomon Islands following flood disaster
4 April 2014
Commonwealth Secretary-General Kamalesh Sharma today conveyed his sympathy to the government and people of Solomon Islands following the loss of life and destruction of property caused by flash floods that struck the Pacific island nation on 3 April 2014. Speaking on behalf of the Commonwealth he offered condolences to those who have been bereaved, and pledged solidarity with all those displaced or otherwise affected by the catastrophe.
