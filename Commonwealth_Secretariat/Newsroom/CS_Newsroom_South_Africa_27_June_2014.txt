Commonwealth releases observer report on South Africa elections
27 June 2014
Commonwealth Secretary-General Kamalesh Sharma today released the report of the observer mission that was present during the National and Provincial elections in South Africa on 7 May 2014. The Commonwealth Observer Mission was led by Nana Dankwa Akufo Addo, former Minister of Foreign Affairs and Attorney General of Ghana. The report has been sent to various stakeholders in South Africa, including the Government, political parties and the Electoral Commission.
