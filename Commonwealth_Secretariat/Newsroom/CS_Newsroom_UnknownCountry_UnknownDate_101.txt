On 22 May 2016, the Commonwealth hosted its annual Health Ministers Meeting in Geneva, under the theme, ‘Health Security and Access to Universal Health Coverage’. The meeting was chaired by Canada's Minister of Health, Jane Philpott. In view of the challenges of unexpected disease outbreaks and climate change, the meeting offered governments an opportunity for a frank exchange on global health security and emergency preparedness in the context of universal health coverage. Ministers also discussed Commonwealth health priorities since the adoption of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) in September 2015.
