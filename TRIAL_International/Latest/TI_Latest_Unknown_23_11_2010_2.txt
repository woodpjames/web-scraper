Elvir Jakupovic
23.11.2010
facts

Elvir Jakupović was born on 29 March 1976.
He was a member of the 1st Company of the 1st Battalion of the 17th Krajina Brigade of the army of Bosnia and Herzegovina (BiH) during the conflict between the army of BiH and the Croatian Defence Council (HVO) which took place between 1992 and 1995.
In June 1993, Jakupović, who at the time was only 17 years old, was deployed in the village of Simena in the Travnik municipality, BiH.
On 9 June 1993, a member of the HVO who was detained by the enemy forces was shot death at gunpoint in Simena.
