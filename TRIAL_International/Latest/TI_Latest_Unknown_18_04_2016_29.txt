Marko Milosevic
18.04.2016
facts

Marko Milosevic was born in 1964 in Lupoglav, in Bosnia-Herzegovina. He was the Deputy commander of Ostoja Stanišić in the Sixth Battalion of the Bosnian Serb Army’s Zvornik Brigade.
Milosevic was accused of knowingly aided the Bosnian Serb Army to displace the Bosnian civil population from the UN security zone of Srebrenica. More than 7’000 Bosnian men and boys were summarily executed.  On 14 and 15 July 1992, under Stanišić command, several hundreds captured men from Srebrenica were transfer to Petkovci and shot dead.
