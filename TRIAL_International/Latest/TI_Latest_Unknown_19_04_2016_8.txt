Kirilas Kurakinas
19.04.2016
facts

Kirilas Kurakinas was born around 1922.
A former official of the Soviet Lithuania’s People’s Commissariat of internal affairs (NKVD, a predecessor of the KGB), he and two other persons of the People’s protection brigade (cf. “ramifications”), have allegedly participated in the murder of a Lithuanian family in hiding from the Soviet Army in 1945. It is alleged that the three of them burned Leopoldas Gaidys’s house, killing four people including his pregnant wife and six-month-old child.
