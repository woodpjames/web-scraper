Célestin Ugirashebuja
31.05.2016
facts

Célestin Ugirashebuja was born in 1953. He is the former mayor of Kigona and member of the National Revolutionary Movement for Democracy.
Ugirashebuja was charged with participating in 5 massacres. Some witnesses have reported that he had his own Interahamwe unit (an extremist Hutu militia group) which, during the second week of April 2004, patrolled the commune pillaging, ransacking and raping women. He was also reported to have organised meetings in order to prepare the massacres and to incite the people present to kill the Tutsis. Ugirashebuja’s role in this region was to ensure that the Tutsi were massacred.
