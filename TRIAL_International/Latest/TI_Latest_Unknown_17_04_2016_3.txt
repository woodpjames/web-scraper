Ostoja Stanisic
17.04.2016
facts

Ostoja Stanišić was born in 1951 in Fojnica, in Bosnia Herzegovina. He was commander of the Sixth Battalion of the Bosnian Serb Army’s Zvornik Brigade.
Stanišić was accused of genocide, in his capacity as commander. He was alleged to have knowingly aided the Bosnian Serb Army to displace the Bosnian civil population from the UN security zone of Srebrenica. More than 7’000 Bosnian men and boys from the enclave were summarily executed. On 14 and 15 July 1992, under Stanišić command, several hundreds captured men from Srebrenica were transfer to Petkovci and shot dead.
