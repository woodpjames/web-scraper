Jean-Pierre Sewabeza
15.04.2016
facts

Jean-Pierre Sewabezawas was mayor of Kagano in the former prefecture of Cyangugu (South West Rwanda) when the events took place. He was also the vice-president in this prefecture of the CDR (Coalition for the Defense of the Republic).
He was indicted for having locked up Tutsis in the communal prison where they were humiliated, beaten and in some cases murdered. He was also charged with having incited the massacre of Tutsis during several gatherings including that of 2 May 1994 which was followed by the massacre of Tutsis in the Catholic parish church in Nyamasheke.
