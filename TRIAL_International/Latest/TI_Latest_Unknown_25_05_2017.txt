Dragoljub Kojic
25.05.2017
Facts

Dragoljub Kojic is a former member of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of Republika Srpska. During the conflict in Bosnia and Herzegovina, he was assigned as a security guard to the forced labour camp of Zasavica, in the Bosanski Samac municipality, where non-Serbs were sent in isolation by the authorities of Republika Srpska.
In July 1992, Kojic allegedly raped a civilian several times in Zasavica. The rape was reportedly committed on at least three occasions, while threatening the victim with a revolver.
