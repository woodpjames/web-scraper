Suad Kapic
13.11.2013
facts 

Kapić Suad was born on 31 July 1975 in Cazin and is a Bosnian Muslim. He studied economics and worked as a bee-farmer. He is married and father of two children.
In 1994 he served in the Army of the Republic of Bosnia and Herzegovina as a corporal. In this capacity Suad Kapić participated in the capture of six members of the Army of the Republika Srpska on 18 September 1995. The captured soldiers were taken to Mreznica Hill where Kapić shot three of them and severely wounded another. Kapić then ordered a soldier from his own ranks to kill the wounded prisoner. The bodies of the murdered men were found at the scene of the crime.
Kapić was taken into custody on 21 September 2007 and his indictment was confirmed on 19 October 2007.
