Ivan Koler
22.07.2016
Facts

Ivan Koler was born in 1972 and is a resident of Tuzla.
He was a member of the military police with the District Headquarters of the Territorial Defence in Tuzla and a guard in the District Military Prison in Tuzla from the beginning of June to the end of August 1992.
