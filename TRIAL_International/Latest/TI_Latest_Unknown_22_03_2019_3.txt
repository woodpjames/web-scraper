Abbas R.
22.03.2019
Facts

Abbas R. allegedly joined ISIS in June 2014 in Mosul. In the same month, he was present during the execution of two Shiite women by ISIS fighters and assisted them with the transportation of the bodies.
In October 2014, Abbas R. allegedly participated in the execution of a high-ranking Iraqi official captured by ISIS. He is also accused of having insulted and humiliated the captive.
