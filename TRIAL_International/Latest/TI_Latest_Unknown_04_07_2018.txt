“They were raping our girls”
04.07.2018
Rémy Cingo is a father from Kavumu, in the Democratic Republic of the Congo. When a militia started raping girls and babies, the authorities turned a blind eye. It was therefore the parents of Kavumu – ordinary citizens – who fought for the opening of a trial. Remy evokes their determination and their thirst for justice, in spite of all the risks.
