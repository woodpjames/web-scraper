Predrag Bajic
08.05.2016
facts 


Predrag Bajic is a Serb. Originally from Gradiska, he is a former member of the Army of Republika Sprska (the VRS). Bajic was stationed in the municipality of Kljuc, where he supposedly committed the crimes of which he is accused. When commiting these crimes, Bajic is said to have been accompanied by his brother, Nenad Bajic, and Sinisa Babic, who were also members of the VRS.
According to Djevad Kumalic, a witness, on the evening of 27 July 1992, Predrag Bajic, accompanied by his brother Nenad and Babic, entered Kumalic’s house. The three men supposedly then asked Kumalic for money, insulted him and fired shots. The three men are said to have been accompanied by other soldiers who have not been identified. Many people died that evening. Another witness, Islemagic, confirmed that the Bajic brothers had entered the house while Babic waited outside.
Another witness, Nermin Halilovic, said that one night in July 1992, he saw two armed men and a man on a motorcycle shoot a civilian, who was later identified as Refjk Puric. Halilovic confirmed that these men were the Bajic brothers and Sinisa Babic. Ratko Marceti, the commander of the military checkpoint in Pudin Han in 1992, accused the Bajic brothers and Babic of many murders in the region
Babic also raped Bosnian civilians.
Predrag Bajic was arrested on 21 February 2013, along with four other people (his brother Nenad Bajic, Mladenko Vrtunic, Milan Lukic and Slobodan Dragic). Sinisa Babic was also arrested in February 2013. Predrag and Nenad Bajic, Milan Lukic,Slobodan Dragic, Mladenko Vrtunic and Sinisa Babic are suspected of having committed other murders, rape and the inhumane treatment of Bosnians in many villages in the municipality of Kljuc.
