Halil Dacaj
02.09.2016
Facts

Halil Dacaj was born in 1974 in Kosovo. During the war he allegedly fought as a Kosovo Liberation Army (KLA) soldier.
On 21 November 1999, Dacaj was admitted into the US as a temporary tourist visitor. However, he entered by presenting a fraudulent passport and visa.
Dacaj applied for asylyum in the US, however during his asylum interview before an immigration judge and an asylum officer, he made incriminating statements revealing his past involvement in the KLA persecution of suspected Serbian collaborators. He is accused of having interrogated, beaten and delivered prisoners to KLA command for further torture.
