Monika Karan-Ilic
15.04.2016
facts

Monika Karan-Ilic was born in 1976. She is associated with Goran Jelisic, himself sentenced to 40 years imprisonment by the ICTY for having killed inmates of the detention camp “Luka” in Brcko.
Karan-Illic was charged with torture, inhumane treatment and the physical abuse of detainees of the detention camp “Luka” and a police station in Brcko, Bosnia and Herzegovina, from the beginning of May to 10 July 1992. The detainees were non-Serb civilians.
She was arrested on 21 December 2011.
