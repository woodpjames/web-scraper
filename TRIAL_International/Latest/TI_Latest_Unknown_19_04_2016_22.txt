Goran Grabez
19.04.2016
facts

Goran Grabez was born in 1965 in Prijedor, in Bosnia-Herzegovina.
During the war in Bosnia, the Serb troops and militia led numerous attacks in the Prijedor region against civilians and especially against Muslims. A large number of civilians was deported to detention camps, where they were subject to inhuman living conditions, torture and extrajudicial executions.
On 17 April 1995, Grabez arrived in Geneva where he asked for political asylum.
