Zoran Tomić
12.04.2016
Facts

Zoran Tomić was born on 3 August 1971 in Stupari, Kladanj Municipality, Bosnia-Herzegovina.
Between 12 July 1995 and 19 July 1995, Tomić was a member of the Special Police Force of the Second Šekovići Detachment (Second Detachment) which operated in the region of Srebrenica.
On 12 and 13 July 1995, Tomić, together with other members of the Second Detachment, was deployed in the immediate vicinity of the village of Kravica, near Srebrenica, and on 13 July 1995 he was in charge of guarding a group of captured Bosnian men in Kravica.
On 13 July 1995, more than 1.000 men were detained and killed in an agricultural warehouse in Kravica.
It is estimated that around 8,000 Bosnian Muslims were massacred in the Srebrenica region between 12 and 19 of July 1995.
