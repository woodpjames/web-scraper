Jorge Antonio Olivera
21.04.2016
facts

Jorge Antonio Olivera is the son of General Olivera Rovere (also facing trial). He is a lawyer and holds the rank of Major in the army.
Between 1975 and 1977, he became lieutenant in the 22nd Mountain Infantry Regiment (RIM 22) near the town of San Juan, Argentina. RIM 22 was a secret militia spying on opponents. The then Lieutenant Jorge Olivera allegedly arrested, tortured, and eliminated the Franco-Argentine Anne Marie Erize Tisseau (24 years old) on 15 October 1976 in San Juan.
