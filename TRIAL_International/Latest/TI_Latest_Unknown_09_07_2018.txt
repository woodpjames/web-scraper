“Prisoners were mistreated and tortured… they were falling like flies”
09.07.2018
Pierre-Claver Mbonimpa spent 2 years in prison in Burundi because of his political views. He experienced torture, humiliation and starvation. Yet from this traumatizing experience, he drew the strength to fight back, through justice and advocacy.
