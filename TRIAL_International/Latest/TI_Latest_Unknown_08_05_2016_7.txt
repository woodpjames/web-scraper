Azem Ibrovic
08.05.2016
facts 


Azem Ibrovic was born on 15 December 1970 in Novi Pazar, Serbia.
He was a police officer within the Ministry of Internal Affairs of Herzegovina-Neretva Canton (MUP HNK) and resident of Jablanica in Bosnia and Herzegovina.
Between 28 and 29 July 1993, during the armed conflict between the Army of Bosnia and Herzegovina and the Croatian Defence Council, Ibrovic allegedly abused and tortured ethnic Croats and members of the Croatian Defence Council who had been detained at the Battle of Neretva Museum in Jablanica.
On 14 December 2011 Ibrovic, Enes Maksumic and Jusuf Hindic were arrested by the State Investigation and Protection Agency (SIPA) in Jablanica. They were released following investigation.
