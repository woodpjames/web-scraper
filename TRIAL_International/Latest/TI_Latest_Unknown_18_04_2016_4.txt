Valentim Lávio
18.04.2016
facts

Valentim Lávio is a former member of the pro-Indonesian “Besi Merah Putih” militia (BMP which literally means “red and white iron”. This group led general and systematic attacks in the Liquiçá area, at the north coast, between January and September 1999.
On 6 September 1999, two days after the results of the referendum for independence for East Timor were announced, Lávio took part in the killing of Patricio Sarmento Viegas, a supporter of independence, in the district of Liquiçá.
Lávio’s trial began in May 2011 before the Dili District Court.
