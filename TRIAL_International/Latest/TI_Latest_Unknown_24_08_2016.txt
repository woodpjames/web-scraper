Goran Pejic
24.08.2016
Facts

Goran Pejic is suspected of killing a family of five Croats, the Ecimovic family, in Tukovi near Prijedor, during the conflict in Ex-Yugoslavia. Five adults were killed in June 1992, while two children escaped. It is alleged that three adults were killed by automatic rifles in a first house, from which the children managed to flee. Afterward, two elderly persons, also from the Ecomovic family were allegedly killed in a second house. These crimes were allegedly committed as part of a systematic attack from the Bosnian Serb army against Croat and Bosnian civilians in Prijedor.
