Sead Velagic
09.05.2016
facts


Sead Velagić was born on 25 October 1966 in Livno, Bosnia and Herzegovina.
During the war agitating Bosnia and Herzegovina in 1992, Velagić was a member of the crime unit of the HVO Military Police in Livno. Velagić was under the command of Zdenko Andabak, then Commander of the Livno Military Police, and Muamir Jasarević, Deputy Commander of that same battalion.
Velagić is accused of having committed several crimes against humanity during his service in 1992 in the municipality of Livno. Indeed, Velagić, along with his two superiors, Andabak and Jasarević, allegedly arrested and detained unlawfully 300 Serb civilians in the school of Ivan Goran Kovacic, located in Livno. Three prisoners died as a result of the inhuman treatment they suffered while 13 others were executed. Their bodies were later found burnt in the vicinity of Livno.
