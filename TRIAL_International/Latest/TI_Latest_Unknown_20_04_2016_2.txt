Petras Bartasevicius
20.04.2016
facts

Petras Bartasevicius was born around 1923.
A former member of the Soviet Lithuania’s People’s Commissariat of internal affairs (NKVD, a predecessor of the KGB), he and two other persons of the People’s protection brigade (cf. ‘related cases’), have allegedly participated in the murder of a Lithuanian family in hiding from the Soviet Army in 1945. It is alleged that the three of them burned Leopoldas Gaidys’s house, killing four people including his pregnant wife and six-month-old child.
