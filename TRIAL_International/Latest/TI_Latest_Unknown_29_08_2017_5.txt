Carlos Humberto Caggiano Tedesco
29.08.2017
Facts

Carlos Humberto Caggiano Tedesco was born on 16 August 1929 in Buenos Aires, Argentina.
From January 1977 to February 1979, Tedesco was a military Colonel of the Argentinian army in charge of ‘area 232’ whose jurisdiction extended to the province of Misiones, in northeast Argentina.
During this time, more than 40 people were arrested and subjected to extreme violence, and 4 people were killed while in detention within ‘area 232’ in furtherance of an inter-governmental plan to persecute leftist dissidents (Operation Condor).
