Zulfikar Alispago
23.04.2016
facts

Zulfikar Alispago was born on 26 December 1958 in Novi Pazar, Serbia. He currently lives in Sarajevo, Bosnia and Herzegovina and has double citizenship of Serbia and Bosnia Herzegovina.
During the war, he was former commander of a special unit known as “Zulfikar” under Supreme Command Staff of the Army of Bosnia and Herzegovina.
His unit is accused of committing war crimes in the village of Trusina, Konjic Municipality, committed on 16 April 1993, where 22 people were killed – 19 civilians and 3 soldiers of the Croatian Army, which had previously surrendered,- wounded 4 people, including 2 children.
