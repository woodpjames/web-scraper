Damir Dosen
19.04.2016
facts

Damir Dosen, alias Kajin, was born in 1967 in the Prijedor municipality. Between June and August 1992, he was a guard shift commander at the Bosnian Serb-run Keraterm camp, supervising a group of 6 to 12 men.
Between 24 May 1992 and 5 August 1992, the detainees, Bosnian Muslim, Bosnian Croat and other non-Serbians civilians, were subjected to inhumane living conditions, including beatings, deprivation of food and water, lack of medical care, overcrowding, as well as absence of adequate toilet facilities. It has also been alleged that murder, physical abuse, rape, sexual assault and various other forms of physical and psychological abuse were used in the camp.
