Slavko Ikanovic
02.09.2016
Facts

Slavko Ikanovic is a former member of the Vucijacka Brigade of the Bosnian Serb Army.
He was charged with having committed war crimes together with Stanji Vukmirovic, who has since passed away, against the Croatian civil population in Derventa, in Bosnia and Herzegovina.
Notably, Ikanovic was found guilty of murdering Anica Andjelic in the village of Gornji Bozinci near Derventa in October 1992. He allegedly stabbed Andjelic with a knife several times, before Vukmirovic shot her dead.
