Mustafa Ahmad Al-Hawsawi
09.05.2016
facts



Mustafa Ahmad al-Hawsawi was born on 5 August 1968 in Jeddah, Saudi Arabia.
In 2000 al-Hawsawi travelled to Afghanistan, allegedly to join the jihad. He received four or five weeks of basic military training in Kabul. After being relocated to Kandahar, he allegedly began working for al-Qaida’s media committee. He served on two other committees: Accounting and Purchasing.
Al-Hawsawi allegedly supported the movement and funding of four of the 9/11 airplane hijackers.
