Petr Kislyiy
31.05.2016
facts

Petr Kislyiy was born in 1921. In March 1949, he was an Inspector in the Ministry of the Interior of the Estonian Soviet Socialist Republic
On 25 March 1949 in his capacity as head of a task force, he participated in the deportation of a family and filled out a questionnaire concerning the family in question and organised the deportation of four persons.
These civilians were deported from the occupied Republic of Estonia to remote areas of the Soviet Union.
