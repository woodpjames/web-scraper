Zeljko Zaric
27.04.2016
facts

Zeljko Zaric, is a Bosnian Serb, born on 3 October 1973 in the village of Slapisnica which lies in the municipality of Bratunac. He was a member of the Serb military police in the Bratunac brigade. He worked in this position together with his co-defendants Zdravko Bozic, Mladen Blagojevic and Zoran Zivanovic.
According to the indictment, during the night of 13 to 14 July 1995, Zeljko Zaric, Zdravko Bozic, Mladen Blagojevic and Zoran Zivanovic were guarding the sites where thousands of Bosnian Muslim civilians were being detained. Over 2’000 to 3’000 people were reportedly detained in the Vuk Karadzic primary school in the town of Bratunac. These detainees came from the Srebrenica enclave. It was alleged that Zeljko Zaric, Zdravko Bozic, Mladen Blagojevic and Zoran Zivanovic abused, beat and inflicted cruel treatment on the detainees.
It was further alleged that on 14 July 1995, Zeljko Zaric, Zdravko Bozic, Mladen Blagojevic and Zoran Zivanovic were on guard at the Grbavci school located in Orahovac in the Zvornic municipality. Here there was said to be held between 1’000 and 2’500 civilian prisoners. They were also reported to have beaten and otherwise inflicted cruel treatment on these prisoners.
Finally, based on the indictment, Zaric and Zivanovic were said to have killed three civilians in Grbavci school on 14 July 1995.
Zelijko Zaric was arrested and detained on 5 December 2006
