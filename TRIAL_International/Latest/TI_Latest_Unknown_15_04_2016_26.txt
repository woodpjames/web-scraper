Max Josef Milde
15.04.2016
facts

Max Josef Milde was born in 20 November 1922, he is German. He was a soldier in the Hermann Göring division of the German army (Wehrmacht) during World War II.
On 29 June 1944 the Hermann Göring division killed 203 civilians in retaliation for a previous attack by partisans on German soldiers. The civilians were citizens from the villages of Civitella, Cornia and San Pancrazio. They had gathered in a church, where most of them were killed by a shot in the neck.
On 10 October 2006, a military tribunal in La Spezia (Italy) tried and convicted Milde in absentia to lifelong imprisonment and the payment of damages to the relatives of nine victims of the massacre.
