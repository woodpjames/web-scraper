Dragan Kolundzija
19.04.2016
facts

Dragan Kolundzija, alias Kole, was born in 1959 in Bosnia-Herzegovina. He was first a guard and then from June to July 1992, he became a shift commander at the Keraterm camp supervising a group of 6 to 12 men.
Between 24 May 1992 and 5 August 1992, the detainees, Bosnian Muslim, Bosnian Croat and other non-Serbians civilians, were subjected to inhumane living conditions, including beatings, deprivation of food and water, lack of medical care, overcrowding as well as the absence of adequate toilet facilities. It has also been alleged that murder, physical abuse, rape, sexual assault and various other forms of physical and psychological abuse were used in the camp.
