Josip Maric
08.05.2016
facts

Josip Maric was born in Kresevo, Bosnia and Herzegovina. He was Police Commander in Kiseljak and Deputy Commander of the Security and Information Unit of the Bosnian Croat Army.
In June 1993, during the conflict between the Bosnian and Croat armies in Bosnia and Herzegovina, Maric allegedly tortured and abused civilians and prisoners of war in Kiseljak, and deported and robbed Bosniak civilians.
The trial against Maric began before the Cantonal Court of Novi Travnik on 7 January 2015.
