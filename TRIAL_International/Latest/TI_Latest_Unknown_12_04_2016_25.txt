Pheneas Munyarugarama
12.04.2016
facts 

Pheneas Munyarugarama was born in Rwanda in 1948.
He is a former military officer of the Republic of Rwanda and is wanted for taking part in the Rwandan genocide which took place between April and July 1994.
In his capacity as a lieutenant Colonel in the Rwandan Army, he allegedly played an essential role in directing the systematic plan aimed at committing genocide and took direct part in carrying out the campaign.
Munyarugarama has fled Rwanda and is believed to live in the Republic of Congo.
Munyarugarama is on the list of ten most wanted suspect of the International Criminal Tribunal for Rwanda (ICTR), that has issued an arrest warrant against Munyarugarama for genocide and war crimes.
