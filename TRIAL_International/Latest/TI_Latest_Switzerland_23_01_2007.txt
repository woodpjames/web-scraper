Switzerland should sign the Convention on Enforced Disappearances promptly
23.01.2007
he Swiss Coalition for the International Criminal Court, whose secretariat TRIAL runs, is asking the Swiss Federal Council to sign the new UN Convention on Enforced Disappearances during the official signature ceremony in Paris, on February 6, 2007.
For more information, see the French and German versions of the website.
