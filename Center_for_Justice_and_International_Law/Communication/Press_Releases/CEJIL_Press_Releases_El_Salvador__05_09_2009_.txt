The Supreme Court of El Salvador orders to investigate the forced disappearance of a child
 05/09/2009 
The Constitutional Chamber of the Supreme Court of El Salvador, when granting a habeas corpus petition, ordered the Attorney General to initiate an investigation to find Sofia García Cruz, a girl victim of a forced disappearance during the armed conflict in the 80s, and to determine the identity of those directly responsible for the crime. (Available in Spanish).
