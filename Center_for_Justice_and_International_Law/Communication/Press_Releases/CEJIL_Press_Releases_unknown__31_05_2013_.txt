43 General Assembly of the OAS
 31/05/2013 
The General Assembly of the OAS will take place from 4 to 6 June 2013 in Antigua, Guatemala. The central theme this year is "For a comprehensive policy against the global drug problem in the Americas."During this period, the General Assembly will elect three Commissioners of the Inter-American Commission on Human Rights (CIDH). Also be submitted for approval various draft resolutions, among which are the remarks to the Annual Report 2012 of the IACHR and the Inter-American Court of Human Rights, as well as draft resolution "Inter-American Convention against Racism, Racial Discrimination and Related Intolerance "and" Inter-American Convention against All Forms of Discrimination and Intolerance. "As part of the 43rd General Assembly of the OAS, the Center for Justice and International Law (CEJIL), the Myrna Mack and the Convergence Foundation for Human Rights in Guatemala, made a public event on "Contributions for Comprehensive Policy on Security "on June 4, 2013 from 2:30 to 5:00 pm at the Hotel Porta in Antigua, Guatemala.
