CEJIL hosts regional consultation with Rashida Manjoo, UN Special Rapporteur on Violence against Women
 30/03/2015 
On March 16th, CEJIL hosted a regional consultation with Rashida Manjoo, UN Special Rapporteur on violence against women on "Bridging the Gap: Should there be a U.N. Convention on violence against women?"  The meeting consisted of an open discussion between the Rapporteur and 30 women's rights defenders from across the hemisphere on the need, advantages, and potential challenges on drafting a UN treaty on violence against women. Additionally, the discussion focused on the region's experiences drafting and implementing the Inter-American Convention on the Prevention, Punishment, and Eradication of Violence against Women, (Convention of Belém do Pará). The meeting was followed by a publicly available webcast and tweet chat on the challenges erradicating violence against women featuring the Rapporteur and Commissioner Tracy Robinson.
