http://Participation of ACDHRS in the 32nd GIMAC Pre-Summit Meeting on Gender Mainstreaming in the African Union
June 20, 2018 to June 21, 2018 
20 – 21 June, 2018 held at Hotel IMAN
Nouakchott, Islamic Republic of MauritaniaThe 32nd GIMAC Pre-Summit Meeting on Gender Mainstreaming in the African Union was held under the guidance of the African Union dedication of the year 2018 to Anti-corruption with a focus on theme “Corruption and Governance: Impact and way out for Women, Children and Youths”.
The meeting took place in Nouakchott, Mauritania on 20-21 June 2018. Recalling on the commitments and recommendations made during the 31st GIMAC held in Addis Ababa in January 2018, the 32nd GIMAC aims to articulate action plans for GIMAC members in view of the new AU reforms. The meeting also afforded GIMAC members to hear from organs of the AU as they share experience on policies, projects and advocacy that has been embarked upon since the 2018 anti-corruption campaigns began.
