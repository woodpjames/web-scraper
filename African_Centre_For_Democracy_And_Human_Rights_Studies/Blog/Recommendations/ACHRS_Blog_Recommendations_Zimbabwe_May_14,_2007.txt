Recommendation on the Protection of Human Rights Defenders during Electoral Period
May 14, 2007
We, the participants meeting at the Forum on the Participation of NGOs in the 41st Ordinary Session of the African Commission on Human and Peoples’ Rights between 12th and 14th May 2007, state as follows:Considering that the situation of human rights defenders are particularly violated during electoral periods;Further considering that States have the obligation to fundamentally ensure the security of every person living on their territory including human rights defenders;Noting the forthcoming elections in Togo, in Guinea, Sierra Leone, in Zimbabwe, in Angola, in Ivory Cost,Deeply concerned that Human Rights Defenders face hostility from political protagonists according to the fact that human rights defenders position is perceived as favouring them or not as it was noticed in Togo, DRC, Nigeria,Recommend that:Done in Accra,  May 14th, 2007The African Centre for Democracy and Human Rights Studies (ACDHRS)Website: www.acdhrs.orgEmail: admin@acdhrs.orgZoe Tembo Building Kerr Serign,
