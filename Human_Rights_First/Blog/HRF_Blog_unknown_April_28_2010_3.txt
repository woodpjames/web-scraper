Guantanamo Bay: a Video Diary
April 28, 2010
Human Rights First's Daphne Eviatar is in Guantanamo Bay to monitor the military commissions hearing for Omar Khadr, the "child soldier" arrested at age 15 and imprisoned at Guantanamo Bay for eight years now.On her last trip to Guantanamo, Daphne shot some footage that we made into this video diary. It reveals some realities of the place and the perspective she gained from her visit.
