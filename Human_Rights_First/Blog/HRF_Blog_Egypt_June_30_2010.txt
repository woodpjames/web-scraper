Policemen Detained in Khaled Saeed Murder Case in Egypt
June 30, 2010
Egyptian press reports that two policemen are being detained for investigations into the use of excessive force against Khaled Saeed, a 28-year old Egyptian man beaten to death outside an Internet cafe in Alexandria.Human Rights First continues to urge accountability for this brutal murder.
