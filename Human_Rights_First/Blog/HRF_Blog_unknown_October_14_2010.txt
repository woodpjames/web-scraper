Child Soldier Faces Trial at Gitmo. Plea Deal in Works.
October 14, 2010
Omar Khadr faces trial next week on allegations that he killed an  American soldier during a firefight in Khost, Afghanistan. Khadr was  only 15years old when he was detained. If tried, he would be the first  child soldier to face a military commission since WWII. Khadr alleges he  was tortured. Human Rights First has monitored his case from its  inception.
