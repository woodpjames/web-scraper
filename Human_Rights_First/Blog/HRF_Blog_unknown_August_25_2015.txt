What are Human Rights to You?
August 25, 2015
We all have a different conception of what human rights mean in our own lives. Taken together, these perspectives weave together to create a vivid picture of human rights throughout the world. That’s why we want to hear what human rights are to you. Fill out this sign, tweet a photo @humanrights1st and use the hashtag #HumanRightsAre. We’ll share it across our global digital community.
