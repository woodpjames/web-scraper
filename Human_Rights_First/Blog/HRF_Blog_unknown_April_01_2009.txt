Russia: Rights Advocate Seriously Hurt in Attack
April 01, 2009
Obama Should Highlight Safety of Activists in Talks with Medvedev
(New York, April 1, 2009)The Russian authorities should investigate the violent attack against Lev Ponomarev, a prominent Russian human rights defender, eight international human rights organizations said today. Ponomarev, 67, was attacked and seriously injured by several unidentified assailants late on the night of March 31, 2009, outside his apartment building in Moscow.
