Mukasey is Wrong: Op-ed in Wall Street Journal ignores the facts
October 19, 2009
In an op-ed published in the Wall Street Journal today, Former Attorney General Michael Mukasey argues that federal courts are not the place to try terrorist suspects.Thing is, Human Rights First has published two in-depth studies - In Pursuit of Justice - of the federal courts' history in trying terrorism cases, and the facts are that our courts can handle these cases.
