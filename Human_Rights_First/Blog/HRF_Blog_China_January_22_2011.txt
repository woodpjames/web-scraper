Tear Down the Great Firewall! (US China Summit)
January 22, 2011
When Chinese President Hu Jintao arrived in the United States this week for the U.S.-China Summit, the White House rolled out the red carpet. And he participated in a joint press conference with President Obama, which opened the Chinese President to much more open and direct questions than he is accustomed to at home.

Podcast produced by Stephen Brownell.
