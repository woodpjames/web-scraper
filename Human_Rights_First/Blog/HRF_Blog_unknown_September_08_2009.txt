Beyond Guantanamo: Expert Panel To Discuss Prosecuting Terrorists And Protecting America
September 08, 2009
Tallahassee, Florida  Eight years after the Sept. 11 terrorist attacks that killed thousands of Americans and precipitated international military operations to eradicate future threats, the United States is at a critical crossroads with regard to how it will hold and prosecute Guantanamo detainees who stand accused of terrorism-related crimes.
