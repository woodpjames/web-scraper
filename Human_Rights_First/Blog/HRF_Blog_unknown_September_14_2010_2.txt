Blueprint to End Contractor Impunity
September 14, 2010
President Obama stated that America's reputation, and its national security interests, have been damaged by contractor abuse. Human Rights First sent the president and his administration our step-by-step plan to establish effective accountability mechanisms for U.S. government contractors working abroad.
