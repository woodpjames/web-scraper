Disappearance Mystery in Thailand: After Five Years, a Fresh Start or Business as Usual?
March 17, 2010
Five years ago a human rights lawyer named  Somchai Neelaphaijit was forced into a car in Bangkok. He was never seen  again, and no-one has been held accountable for disappearance. A new  government promises action, but will it be business as usual? Please  call on the Thai authorities to solve this case and prevent further  disappearances.
