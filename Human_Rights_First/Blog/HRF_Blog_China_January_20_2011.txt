Elisa Massimino Talks China and Human Rights on Fox News
January 20, 2011
Chinese President Hu Jintao stated yesterday that "he didn't hear" a first question on human rights asked at a White House press conference.

Commenting on Fox News, Human Rights First's Elisa Massimino asks, "Could there be a more striking metaphor for China and human rights?"
