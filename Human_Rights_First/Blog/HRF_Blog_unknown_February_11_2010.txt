9/11 Trials: Elisa Massimino on Brian Lehrer Show
February 11, 2010
Brian Lehrer covered the 9/11 trials in his radio show today, interviewing Human Rights First's President and CEO Elisa Massimino.Massimino underlined that civilian courts are the right place to try suspected terrorists such as Khalid Sheikh Mohammed - and that trying terrorists in federal courts actually gives us an advantage in the fight against Al Qaeda.Listen to her explain why.
