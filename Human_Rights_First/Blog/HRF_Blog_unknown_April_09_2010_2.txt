New Claims that Bush Officials Knew "Vast Majority" of Guantanamo Detainees Were Innocent Underscores Need to Shutter Facility
April 09, 2010
Washington, DC  Colonel Lawrence Wilkerson, former Chief of Staff to Secretary of State Colin Powell, said that Vice President Dick Cheney and Secretary of Defense Donald Rumsfeld knew that the "vast majority" of detainees held at Guantanamo were innocent, according to a story published in The Times of London.
