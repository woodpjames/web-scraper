Times Square Bomber Gets Life
October 05, 2010
A New York court has sentenced Faisal Shahzad, the Times Square  bomber, to life in prison. Contrary to claims by detractors, reading  Shahzad his Miranda rights and trying him in federal courts works well.  In fact, more than 400 other terrorists have been convicted in our  federal court system since 9/11 (compared to 4 in military commissions).  It is time to close Guantanamo.
