New Evidence that Civilian Courts Can Handle Terrorism Cases
March 26, 2010
The latest evidence that the Department of Justice can successfully prosecute terrorism cases is in: a new Justice Department chart confirms that hundreds of terrorism-related convictions in civilian courts since 9/11. Most of the data was compiled by the Bush Administration Justice Department.This chart reaffirms what Human Rights First concluded in its In Pursuit of Justice reports: our courts can handle these cases.
