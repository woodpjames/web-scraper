Hidden Justice in Afghanistan
September 18, 2009
Check out this piece  in the AfPak Channel of Foreign Policy written by Human Rights First's Sahr MuhammedAlly, who speaks of the recent Bagram reforms and next steps.Sahr has conducted comprehensive research and interviews with former Bagram detainees in two separate missions.  In this opinion piece, Sahr discusses further steps the U.S. government can take to address the broad range of problems associated with detentions in Afghanistan to avoid arbitrary detention and reverse the trend of decreasing support to U.S. military operations there.Read her commentary!
