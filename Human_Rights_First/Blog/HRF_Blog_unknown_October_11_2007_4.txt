Poll: 42% of Americans Believe the U.S. Is Torturing Prisoners
October 11, 2007
According to a poll conducted by Rasmussen, 42% of Americans believe the United States currently tortures prisoners captured in the fight against terrorism.  30% disagree and say the U.S. does not torture prisoners while 28% are not sure.  53% say that the U.S. should not torture prisoners while 27% believe it should.
