Human Rights First Statement on the 8th Anniversary of September 11
September 11, 2009
Today we mark the eighth anniversary of the terrorist attacks of September 11, 2001, tragic events that resulted in the deaths of nearly 3000 people and sparked international conflicts that have since claimed thousands of additional lives. We express our heartfelt condolences to the families of the victims, as well as our unwavering support for the men and women who continue to guard our freedom and security at home and abroad.
