Human Rights First CEO Elisa Massimino's Idea in the Atlantic
July 01, 2009
Human Rights First CEO Elisa Massimino is attending the Ideas Festival in Aspen. Her big idea, as presented in an article in the Atlantic, is:America's "new" policy on rules of war and treatment of prisoners should beits original policy, created by George Washington and expressed in the GenevaConventions and saying that there are certain lines that can never becrossed.
