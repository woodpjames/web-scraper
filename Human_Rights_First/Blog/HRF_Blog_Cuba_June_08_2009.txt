Release Ill Cuban Activist from Isolation Cell
June 08, 2009
Tony Diaz, an instrumental organizer of a  civic initiative known as the Varela Project in Cuba, was thrown in jail  for his human rights activities in the spring of 2003.  This week, Diaz  was transferred to a prison isolation cell, seriously endangering his  health.                Read More about Tony Diaz's Case
