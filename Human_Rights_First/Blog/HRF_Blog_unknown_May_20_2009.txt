Iranian Human Rights Defender Wins 2009 Martin Ennals Award
May 20, 2009
Emad Baghi, a leading human rights defender  based in Tehran, founded the Society for the Defense of Prisoners'  Rights and has been a vigorous and outspoken opponent of the death  penalty in Iran.  The Martin Ennals Award, a joint award from ten  international rights groups, recognizes his vital work.               Read Press Release
