The Jack Bauer Contagion
July 29, 2008
The fictional “24” character Jack Bauer has not only been heralded a hero of counterterrorism within the world of TV, but he has also been  the basis for U.S. interrogation policy. Diane Beaver, John Yoo, Michael Chertoff and Antonin Scalia have all looked to Bauer for advice. Yet, he’s fictional.       To find out more about the devastating effects of Jack Bauer's influence, check out HRF's Primetime Torture project.
