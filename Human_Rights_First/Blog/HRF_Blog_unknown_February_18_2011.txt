On Torture and Interrogations
February 18, 2011
Jim Cullen, one of our key partners from the retired military who have spoken out against torture and Guantanamo, published a letter in the New York Law Journal beginning:
As a retired brigadier general in the U.S. Army Judge Advocate General's corps, I read with interest the article about Michael Mukasey's continued attacks on the Obama Administrations antiterrorism policies, including trials of terrorists in federal courts, while he endorses waterboarding...
Read the letter (subscription required) and watch videos of other retired military leaders urging that we try terrorist suspects--including Khalid Sheikh Mohammed--in federal courts. And sign our petition to close Guantanamo.
