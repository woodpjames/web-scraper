Cheney 'persists in defending these disgraceful policies ... '
December 21, 2008
As quoted in a Washington Post article today looking at Vice President Cheney's record, HRF CEO and Executive Director Elisa Massimino said Cheney:"persists in defending these disgraceful policies of abuse which have been rejected by senior retired military leaders and experienced interrogators as ineffective and counterproductive."Human Rights First has been working with a group of retired military officers in an effort to end official cruelty and restore America's commitment to the rule of law.
