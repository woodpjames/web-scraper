Urge G8 Leaders to Call for Release of All Detained Activists in Iran
July 07, 2009
Hundreds of people have been detained in Iran  since the June 12th announcement of the disputed presidential election  results.  The detainees include prominent political leaders who opposed  President Ahmadinejad during the campaign, student leaders, journalists,  human rights lawyers and women's rights activists.
