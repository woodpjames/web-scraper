HRF Urges Human Rights Council to Protect Human Rights Defenders
March 10, 2010
Senior Associate, Andrew Hudson, addressed the  Human Rights Council in Geneva on March 11, 2010 urging it to better  protect human rights defenders at risk around the world. He also urged  the Colombian government to adopt the Council's recommendations on how  to stop the persecution of defenders.               HRF Oral Statement to Council
