// ]]>In 1776, Americans declared independence based on a set of ideals: our core freedoms. These ideals are universal human rights. At Human Rights First, we believe that America is strongest when our policies and actions match our values. This is why we push the United States to lead on human rights. For us, this means fighting terrorism without sacrificing our values, protecting the persecuted, preventing mass atrocities, and standing with others around the world who demand their freedom.
