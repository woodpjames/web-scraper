#Syria in Crisis [Timeline]
April 13, 2012
As the delicate ceasefire in Syria holds, we at Human Rights First continue our work, urging the Friend of Syria countries and Syrian allies like Russia and Iran to call on President Assad to maintain the agreement. We are also urging Russian arms exporter Rosoboronexport to immediately stop its sale and deliver of weapons to Syria. The ceasefire is not remedy enough to end the atrocities in Syria.
