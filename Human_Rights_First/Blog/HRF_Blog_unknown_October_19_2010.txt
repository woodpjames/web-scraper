Human Rights First Contributes to the 2010 Review Conference
October 19, 2010
For the first time in 11 years, the Organization for Security and  Cooperation in Europe (OSCE) convened a review conference, in advance of  the heads of state summit scheduled for December 1-2 in Astana,  Kazakhstan. Human Rights First made interventions during sessions on  fundamental rights, tolerance and nondiscrimination, and migrant rights.
